# Pentex™ Subversion

> Card text pending krcg.org ingest.

— [krcg.org entry](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Pentex+Subversion)

## Why it has a page
Pentex Subversion pulls rulings from three distinct mechanics at once: retainers, directed actions against masters, and the "can't block but can react" distinction. Its FAQ is frequently mis-applied in playgroups.

## Rulings
- **The bearing minion cannot burn the Pentex themselves.** Only **other** minions — including those controlled by the Methuselah who played the card, or any other Methuselah — can perform the action to burn it [src-001 p. 50].
- **The burn action is directed against the Methuselah who played the Pentex Subversion**, not against the Methuselah controlling the bearing minion [src-001 p. 50]. Master cards are considered controlled by the Methuselah who played them.
- **A minion who cannot block can still play reaction cards** such as [Deflection](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Deflection) [src-001 p. 50]. Being under Pentex's "cannot block" effect does not strip reaction-card capability.

## Interactions
- **With [[employ-retainer]]:** the card effectively attaches like a retainer but acts against the bearer; unlike a proper retainer, it can't be burned by its bearer.
- **With [[block-resolution]]:** the "can react but not block" distinction is the same rule that applies to a locked-and-woken vampire playing Deflection.
- **With [[target-redirection]]:** a bleed redirected onto a Pentex'd vampire's controller still follows normal redirect rules; Pentex doesn't grant any extra redirect option.

## Common Mistakes
- Assuming the Pentex-bearer's controller can burn it — they cannot; the bearer is on their own team but can't shed it.
- Directing the burn action against the bearer's controller — wrong; the action is directed against the Pentex-playing Methuselah.
- Forgetting the bearer can still play reactions — they keep Deflection, Telepathic Misdirection, etc., even if they can't block.
