# Vessel

> Card text pending krcg.org ingest.

— [krcg.org entry](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Vessel)

## Why it has a page
Vessel has an unusual cross-Methuselah split: the Methuselah who played it retains **control** of the master card, while the target vampire (even one controlled by a different Methuselah) is the **bearer** who uses it. Combined with Blood Doll timing, this causes regular disputes.

## Rulings

### Blood Doll interaction
- Burning a [Blood Doll](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Blood%20Doll) as part of playing Vessel is **optional** [src-001 p. 51].
- If you choose to burn a Blood Doll, you must **announce it when playing the card** — *before* replacing the card. You cannot decide later [src-001 p. 51].

### Master-phase timing
- The card is played during the **master phase**. You must wait **until next turn** to start moving blood with it [src-001 p. 51]. Contrast with [Blood Doll](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Blood%20Doll), which **can** be used on the turn it's played [src-001 p. 47].

### Cross-Methuselah placement
- Vessel can be played on a vampire controlled by **another Methuselah** [src-001 pp. 51–52].
- In that case: **you still control the Vessel card**, but **only the controller of the vampire with it can use it** [src-001 pp. 51–52].
- Practical consequence: the Vessel counts as your master card for limit purposes, but the other Methuselah reaps the blood-movement benefit.

## Interactions
- **With [[master-phase]]:** the "wait until next turn" rule matches Anarch Troublemaker, Vessel, and Arcane Library/Art Museum.
- **With Blood Doll:** see the dedicated interaction above.
- **With [[unlock-phase]]:** the optional blood-move happens in the unlock phase of the controller of the bearing vampire.

## Common Mistakes
- Deciding mid-play to burn a Blood Doll — no; the decision is at announcement, before replace.
- Moving blood on the turn Vessel was played — not allowed.
- Placing Vessel on another Methuselah's vampire expecting they then control Vessel — they control *use*, not the card itself.
