# Malk' 22

V5 Malkavian **stealth-and-bleed** archetype — modern ("2022-era") descendant of the classic **Malk' 94**. The deck has **zero combat cards** and runs the bleed race purely on Dominate bleed amplification + Obfuscate stealth (roughly one-third of the library is stealth modifiers or block-denial). Two-phase plan: [Govern the Unaligned](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Govern%20the%20Unaligned) × 17 speeds out 4 vampires, then switch gears into hard Obfuscate-stealth bleeds amplified by [Conditioning](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Conditioning) × 7. Codex Top Tier [src-012].

Canonical decklist: **Arnau Diez Sans**, Nacional VTES España 2023 — Sevilla, 2023-03-25, TWDA id 10419 [src-012].

## Core idea

1. **Ramp phase.** [Govern the Unaligned](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Govern%20the%20Unaligned) × 17 is the densest concentration in the deck. Every Govern at inferior influences a cheaper/smaller vampire; at superior it's a bleed mode. Aim for 4 vampires before switching to attack phase.
2. **Attack phase.** Pure bleed: every action is a bleed + stealth stack. [Conditioning](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Conditioning) × 7 (+1 bleed DOM, unpreventable by non-DOM/PRE reactions), [Seduction](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Seduction) × 4 (DOM; bleed uses vampire's blood instead of prey's blood — bypasses Direct Intervention logic and some reactions), [Bonding](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Bonding) × 3 (+1 stealth + vampire gains the printed card; chains with Deflection/Seduction).
3. **Stealth stack.** [Spying Mission](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Spying%20Mission) × 7 (+1 stealth, cycles free card draw), [Lost in Crowds](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Lost%20in%20Crowds) × 5, [Cloak the Gathering](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Cloak%20the%20Gathering) × 4, [Elder Impersonation](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Elder%20Impersonation) × 4 (stealth + cycle), [Faceless Night](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Faceless%20Night) × 3 ("cannot back out" if not blocked), [Swallowed by the Night](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Swallowed%20by%20the%20Night) × 3 (action-mod / combat).
4. **Block-denial riders.** [Hide the Mind](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Hide%20the%20Mind) × 3 (OBF; cancels an AUS reaction or second-block-attempt), [Pentex™ Subversion](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Pentex%E2%84%A2%20Subversion), [Misdirection](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Misdirection) (Master — a blocker cannot re-attempt).
5. **Defence.** [Deflection](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Deflection) × 6 is the primary defence; [Delaying Tactics](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Delaying%20Tactics) × 2 anti-vote; [On the Qui Vive](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=On%20the%20Qui%20Vive) × 3 wake/unlock reaction; [My Enemy's Enemy](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=My%20Enemy%27s%20Enemy) × 1, [Telepathic Misdirection](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Telepathic%20Misdirection) × 1. **Zero combat cards.**
6. **Anti-bloat / disruption.** [Wash](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Wash) × 3 ("a bleed in disguise" — burn a prey's non-unique master); [Anarch Troublemaker](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Anarch%20Troublemaker) tax hand size.
7. **Don't prematurely oust.** Spying Mission and Hide the Mind buffer against accidentally ousting a non-prey opponent via Deflection bounces (Codex tip).

## Support / tech

### Bleed amp
Conditioning × 7 is the flagship +1 bleed DOM. Seduction × 4 bypasses Direct Intervention-class cancellation by using the vampire's own blood. Foreshadowing Destruction × 2 strips a prey's bonus card text pre-bleed.

### Stealth density
30+ cards are pure stealth or stealth-carrying modifiers. Against typical intercept density (2 Organized Resistance + 2 On the Qui Vive), the +2/+3 stealth on every bleed outpaces blocks.

### Block denial
Hide the Mind (kills AUS reactions), Misdirection (permanent block-fail on the first successful block), Foreshadowing Destruction (removes bonus card text from a card played on the prey side).

### No combat
Zero combat cards. Deck concedes all combats — design choice: combat cards don't advance the bleed race. Predators that rush multiple times / successfully to torpor can stall the deck.

### Bloat-disruption
Wash × 3 acts as a "masked bleed": burn a prey's unique-looking but burnable master (Villein, Minion Tap, etc.) to delay their setup. Anarch Troublemaker costs predator a free card-cycle.

## Variants

- **Malk' 94 (classic)** — ancestor; similar but with legacy card pool (pre-V5 Malkavians).
- **Combat-defence add** — include 4–6 Earth Meld / Forced Awakening / Telepathic Misdirection for a softer matchup vs rush.
- **Toolbox** — add weapons (Sniper Rifle) and light politics (Kine Resources Contested) for flexibility.

## Weaknesses

1. **Rush predators annihilate it.** Zero combat cards. Tupdogs, Illegal Brawl, Gangrel Brutal send vampires to torpor with no rebuttal.
2. **Wall preys.** Heavy intercept (Gangrel Thing × 9 Organized Resistance + Deep Ecology) can outstack +1 stealth despite volume.
3. **DOM-mirror bounce.** Predators on DOM bounce (Deflection) can reroute bleeds into grand-prey.
4. **Archon Investigation / Diablerie tech** — a long game lets predators setup Archon bait; the deck has no way to beat a well-set Archon.
5. **Minion Tap absent.** No blood-to-pool; blood economy runs mostly on Govern influence.
