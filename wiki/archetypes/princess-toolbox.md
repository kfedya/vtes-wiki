# Princess Toolbox

Tremere **intercept-wall toolbox** built around [Carna, The Princess Witch](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/crypt/index.html?card=Carna%2C%20The%20Princess%20Witch) (cap 8, `AUS/DOM/THA`, Tremere). The deck packs the densest reaction suite in top-tier play (**39 reactions**: Deflection × 8, Eyes of Argus × 8, Enhanced Senses × 4, Eagle's Sight × 4, Telepathic Misdirection × 4, On the Qui Vive × 3, Forced Awakening × 3, Delaying Tactics × 3, My Enemy's Enemy × 2) and grinds via [Theft of Vitae](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Theft%20of%20Vitae) × 4 combat (steal blood from the opposing vampire on every block). Codex Top Tier [src-015].

Canonical decklist: **Marius Iscru**, Swiss NC 2023, 2023-07-02, TWDA id 10550 [src-015].

## Core idea

1. **Seat [Carna, The Princess Witch](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/crypt/index.html?card=Carna%2C%20The%20Princess%20Witch) × 4 first.** Cap-8 with triple Tremere `AUS/DOM/THA` superior discipline. Enables the full intercept + bounce + combat stack on one vampire.
2. **[Magic of the Smith](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Magic%20of%20the%20Smith) × 6.** THA action, `[DOM][THA]` fetches an equipment from library; primary target is [Heart of Nizchetus](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Heart%20of%20Nizchetus) for card filtering, then situationally [Bowl of Convergence](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Bowl%20of%20Convergence) (anti-rush), [Sniper Rifle](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Sniper%20Rifle) (grind combat), [.44 Magnum](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=.44%20Magnum) (maneuver + damage), [Ivory Bow](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Ivory%20Bow), [Kevlar Vest](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Kevlar%20Vest), [Sawed-Off Shotgun](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Sawed-Off%20Shotgun), [Sport Bike](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Sport%20Bike).
3. **Wall.** 39 reaction cards: Deflection × 8 (DOM bounce), Eyes of Argus × 8 (AUS intercept, requires titled or cap-10+), Enhanced Senses × 4 + Eagle's Sight × 4 (AUS intercept), Telepathic Misdirection × 4 (AUS bounce), On the Qui Vive × 3, Forced Awakening × 3 (wake — action-tax on the prey), Delaying Tactics × 3, My Enemy's Enemy × 2. The deck *does not need* to lunge — bounces turn every prey-bleed into a 1-or-2-pool prey hit.
4. **Grind combat via [Theft of Vitae](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Theft%20of%20Vitae) × 4.** THA ranged strike — steal 2 blood (or life) from the opposing minion. Every block becomes a 2-blood drain on the prey.
5. **Bloat.** [Vessel](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Vessel) × 6 on Carna stacks — up to 6 blood/turn to pool. [The Rack](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=The%20Rack) × 2 and [Smiling Jack, The Anarch](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Smiling%20Jack%2C%20The%20Anarch) × 3 tax the prey 1 pool per turn.
6. **Finish with [Conditioning](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Conditioning) × 2** — paired with [Anonymous Freight](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Anonymous%20Freight) × 2 (action: +2 stealth bleed) for the 3–4 bleed lunge when the prey is soft.

## Support / tech

### Reaction stack breakdown
- Bounce: 12 (Deflection × 8 + Telepathic Misdirection × 4).
- Intercept: 19 (Eyes of Argus × 8 + Enhanced Senses × 4 + Eagle's Sight × 4 + My Enemy's Enemy × 2 + On the Qui Vive × 3 as wake+block).
- Wake: 3 (Forced Awakening).
- Vote defence: 3 (Delaying Tactics).

### Equipment tutor chain
Magic of the Smith × 6 → Heart of Nizchetus (card filter) → Sniper Rifle (grind damage) or Bowl of Convergence (anti-rush) depending on matchup.

### Combat
Theft of Vitae × 4 (ranged) + Rego Motum × 4 (maneuver) + Sniper Rifle (grind). No aggressive combat — everything is defensive.

### Eagle's Sight nuance
At `[AUS]` Eagle's Sight lets this vampire block any action regardless of target/predator/prey restrictions. See [[mechanics-index/ignore-block-restrictions|ignore-block-restrictions]] — the deck exploits this to intervene cross-table.

## Variants

- **Toolbox (main)** — with [Govern the Unaligned](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Govern%20the%20Unaligned) added for faster ramp and stealthier bleeds.
- **Royalty** — Princes + Justicars + heavier political actions.
- **Infernal** — [Valerius](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/crypt/index.html?card=Valerius) (Infernal Tremere) instead of Carna — aggravated-damage combat.
- **V5** — newer Tremere (G6+) vampires.
- **Oliver** — [Oliver Thrace](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/crypt/index.html?card=Oliver%20Thrace) to counter Form of Mist / Earth Meld escape.

## Weaknesses

1. **Slow oust.** No bleed engine beyond Conditioning × 2 + Anonymous Freight × 2 + Smiling Jack. Wins by outlasting rather than ousting.
2. **Stealth-heavy predators** bypass intercept when stacked against multiple +1 stealth modifiers.
3. **Event / master removal.** Pentex-on-Carna or Wash-on-Vessel collapses the economy.
4. **Combat-ender redirects.** [Rötschreck](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=R%C3%B6tschreck) on a blocking Carna sends her to torpor, breaking the combat-reaction wall.
5. **No true win condition without prey decline.** Requires stalling to 5-hour timer or prey-self-oust via bounces; in short-round formats the deck can draw.
