# Lasombra Nocturn

Lasombra **stealth-and-bleed** archetype using OBL (Oblivion) discipline. Signature: **[Nocturn](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Nocturn) × 12** — Obtenebration-themed ally with dual use (+bleed damage or combat protection). Pair with [Govern the Unaligned](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Govern%20the%20Unaligned) × 12 for mass influence and Oblivion stealth modifiers. Deck can land 7-damage bleeds by prioritising bleed amp over stealth volume. Codex Runner-up [src-024].

Canonical decklist: **Eduardo García**, Mexican NC 2023, 2023-11-26, TWDA id 11066 [src-024].

## Core idea

1. **Seat Lasombra.** [Alicia Cortez](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/crypt/index.html?card=Alicia%20Cortez) × 2, [Conrad Adoula](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/crypt/index.html?card=Conrad%20Adoula) × 2, [Badr, Shadow of Granada](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/crypt/index.html?card=Badr%2C%20Shadow%20of%20Granada) × 2, + singletons (Lord Vauxhall, Onaedo, Virginie Prodigy, Andrew Emory, Ermenegildo The Rake, Lucy Markowitz).
2. **Ramp with Govern × 12.**
3. **Recruit [Nocturn](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Nocturn) × 12.** OBL ally; each Nocturn can boost a bleed +1 or protect a vampire from combat damage — flexible role.
4. **Bleed amp.** [Conditioning](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Conditioning) × 4 (DOM +1 bleed), [Empowering the Puppet King](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Empowering%20the%20Puppet%20King) × 4 (DOM +X bleed for blood), [Command of the Beast](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Command%20of%20the%20Beast) × 4 (DOM; bleed mode), [Foreshadowing Destruction](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Foreshadowing%20Destruction) × 2 — 14 bleed amps.
5. **Stealth via OBL.** [Shadow Play](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Shadow%20Play) × 6, [Shroud of Absence](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Shroud%20of%20Absence) × 4, [Tenebrous Form](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Tenebrous%20Form) × 2 — 12 stealth.
6. **Defence.** [Deflection](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Deflection) × 8 (DOM bounce), [On the Qui Vive](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=On%20the%20Qui%20Vive) × 6, [Redirection](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Redirection) × 2 (OBL redirect), [Pulling Strings](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Pulling%20Strings) × 1, [Delaying Tactics](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Delaying%20Tactics) × 1.
7. **Combat.** [Oubliette](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Oubliette) × 4 (OBL combat), [Entombment](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Entombment) × 2 (OBL strike → torpor), [Shadow Body](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Shadow%20Body) × 2 (combat ends).
8. **Economy.** [Vessel](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Vessel) × 2, [Blood Doll](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Blood%20Doll) × 2, [Political Hunting Ground](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Political%20Hunting%20Ground), [Information Highway](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Information%20Highway), [The Path of Night](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=The%20Path%20of%20Night) × 2, [Elysian Fields](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Elysian%20Fields).

## Key disciplines and clans

- **Clan:** Lasombra.
- **Disciplines:** `[DOM]` Dominate (Conditioning, Deflection, Govern, Command of the Beast, Empowering the Puppet King), `[OBL]` Oblivion / OBT Obtenebration (Shadow Play, Shroud of Absence, Tenebrous Form, Nocturn, Oubliette, Entombment, Shadow Body, Redirection), `[POT]` splash.
- **Sect:** Mixed Camarilla/Sabbat/Independent per vampire — V5 Lasombra are Camarilla-faction primarily.

## Variants

- **Brutal** — Arms of the Abyss + Entombment replace Nocturn for combat.
- **Black Hand** — G3/G4 vampires for Black Hand support.
- Related archetype: Kiasyd Stealth & Bleed (legacy Lasombra-Fae bloodline variant).

## Weaknesses

1. **Dominate-mirror.** DOM bounce gets re-routed into DOM preys.
2. **Ally-hate tech.** Pentex on Nocturn, Frontal Assault.
3. **Rush.** Oubliette + Shadow Body + Entombment × 2 — enough to survive but won't outpace concerted rush.
4. **Combat-enders.** Rötschreck on a key vampire.
