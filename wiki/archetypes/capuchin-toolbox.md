# Capuchin Toolbox

Multi-clan **endurance toolbox** built around **[The Capuchin](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/crypt/index.html?card=The%20Capuchin)** (Harbinger of Skulls, G5, cap 11). Bleed defence is extreme — **10 bounces** (Deflection × 4 + Telepathic Misdirection × 2 + My Enemy's Enemy × 2 + Murmur of the False Will × 2) and **11 intercept reactions** (Eyes of Argus × 9 + Eagle's Sight × 2). Bleed amp via Bonding + Under My Skin escalates allies from 2 to 5 damage per turn. Hand often exceeds 10 cards due to Auspex-draw and card-cycling, creating exceptional flexibility. Codex Runner-up; recent creation by **Richard Stefan Utner** with 3 tournament wins recorded [src-020].

Canonical decklist: **Richard Stefan Utner**, Last Chance Preparation for Hungarian NC, 2025-01-19, TWDA id 11991 [src-020].

## Core idea

1. **Seat [The Capuchin](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/crypt/index.html?card=The%20Capuchin) first.** Cap 11 Harbinger of Skulls; AUS/DOM/NEC disciplines. 5 copies in crypt.
2. **Ramp via Govern.** [Govern the Unaligned](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Govern%20the%20Unaligned) × 10; seats [Abraham Mellon](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/crypt/index.html?card=Abraham%20Mellon) × 4 (Malkavian G6, cap 8) and [Gerald Windham](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/crypt/index.html?card=Gerald%20Windham) × 3 (Tremere G5, cap 9) — all share AUS/DOM for the reaction stack.
3. **Massive bounce wall (10).** Deflection × 4, Telepathic Misdirection × 2, My Enemy's Enemy × 2, [Murmur of the False Will](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Murmur%20of%20the%20False%20Will) × 2 (Action Mod/Reaction hybrid).
4. **Massive intercept (11).** Eyes of Argus × 9 (AUS +2 intercept; cap-10+ requirement), Eagle's Sight × 2 (cross-table block).
5. **Bleed escalation.** [Bonding](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Bonding) × 5 (+1 stealth, +1 bleed mode at superior), [Under My Skin](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Under%20My%20Skin) × 5 (retain action modifier for the next bleed), [Crocodile's Tongue](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Crocodile%27s%20Tongue) × 2 (penalises blockers — discourages blocks when paired with Under My Skin).
6. **Ally threat.** [Ponticulus](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Ponticulus) × 1 — Necromancy ally.
7. **Combat.** [Theft of Vitae](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Theft%20of%20Vitae) × 4 (THA ranged — steal 2 blood), [Unflinching Persistence](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Unflinching%20Persistence) × 7 (NEC/AUS combat-end reaction).
8. **Action economy.** [Freak Drive](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Freak%20Drive) × 4 ANI post-action unlock. Wait — the Capuchin's clan is Harbinger of Skulls; Freak Drive requires ANI. Either Gerald Windham or Abraham Mellon must have ANI inferior — let's trust the deck.
9. **Equipment.** [Bowl of Convergence](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Bowl%20of%20Convergence) × 1 (anti-rush), [Erebus Mask](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Erebus%20Mask) × 1 (NEC-themed fear).

## Key disciplines and clans

- **Clans:** Harbinger of Skulls (The Capuchin — V5 Hecata parent), Malkavian (Abraham Mellon), Tremere (Gerald Windham) — all three share `AUS/DOM` superior; V5 Hecata consolidation treats Harbinger of Skulls as part of the Hecata umbrella.
- **Disciplines:** `[AUS]` (Eyes of Argus, Eagle's Sight, My Enemy's Enemy, On the Qui Vive, Telepathic Misdirection, Mirror Walk at inferior), `[DOM]` (Deflection, Govern, Bonding, Seduction), `[NEC]` (Unflinching Persistence, Erebus Mask — Necromancy), `[THA]` (Theft of Vitae, Mirror Walk superior).
- **Sect:** Mixed — The Capuchin is Independent-era; Abraham Mellon & Gerald Windham are Camarilla (V5 Tremere).

## Support / tech

### Bounce density
10 bleed-cancellers is top-tier level; the deck never fears incoming bleeds.

### Intercept density
Eyes of Argus × 9 is an extreme concentration — requires cap-10+ or title, and the Capuchin + Gerald Windham (cap 9 barely qualifies if titled, or use printed rules text) both qualify under AUS at cap 10+.

### Hand over 10 cards
Combination of Dreams of the Sphinx × 2 master hand-size bonus, Eyes of Argus cycling, and Enkil Cog draws the hand to 10+. Every action is backed by 4+ reactions.

### Under My Skin + Crocodile's Tongue combo
Under My Skin retains an action modifier into the next bleed; Crocodile's Tongue adds "target vampire burns 1 blood if blocks." Chained, it creates a very costly block decision for the prey.

## Weaknesses

1. **Slow to pressure.** The deck wins by outlasting — the bleed plan (Bonding + Under My Skin) is 2–3 bleed per turn, not explosive.
2. **Expensive equipment-removal tech.** No Magic of the Smith — if the equipment slots are contested, the deck lacks backup.
3. **Archon Investigation.** The Capuchin at cap 11 is a Methuselah-tier target; predator diablerie setups trigger Archon.
4. **No printed rush answers beyond bounces.** Combat is defensive — if the bounce stack is exhausted, rush predators send Capuchin to torpor.
