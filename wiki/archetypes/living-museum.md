# Living Museum

Tzimisce **stealth-and-bleed** archetype centred on the **[Living Manse](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Living%20Manse) × 7 + [The British Museum, London](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=The%20British%20Museum%2C%20London) × 5** engine. Living Manse is a VIC equipment-location (does not count as equipment for other cards, +1 bleed, burn to end combat); British Museum unlocks any minion *after* a successful equip with a non-electronic non-vehicle non-weapon equipment. Chain equips (Living Manse, retainers via the in-play loophole, Heart of Nizchetus) to unlock the same vampire multiple times per turn, then bleed at +1 per Manse on top. Codex Top Tier. Archetype invented by **Darby Keeney** — the "Darby dance" is the NRA PAC / British Museum / unlock timing trick described below [src-010].

Canonical decklist: **Keith Lim**, 2024 AU/NZ Championship, 2025-03-10, TWDA id 11686 [src-010].

## Core idea

1. **Seat an Anarch Tzimisce Baron first.** [Adrino Manauara](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/crypt/index.html?card=Adrino%20Manauara) × 2, [Ángel Guerrero](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/crypt/index.html?card=%C3%81ngel%20Guerrero) × 2, [Neserian](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/crypt/index.html?card=Neserian) × 2, [Branimira](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/crypt/index.html?card=Branimira) × 2 form the core.
2. **Influence with Govern.** [Govern the Unaligned](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Govern%20the%20Unaligned) × 8 speeds subsequent crypt-ramps.
3. **Drop [The British Museum, London](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=The%20British%20Museum%2C%20London) × 5.** Unique location: +1 hand size; any minion can unlock once per turn after a successful non-electronic/non-vehicle/non-weapon equip action.
4. **Spam [Living Manse](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Living%20Manse) × 7.** Each Manse equipped triggers British Museum → unlock → another action. The Manse gives +1 bleed and a burn-to-end-combat safety valve. Only one Manse per vampire, so hand out Manses across the table then pivot to non-equipment actions for the bleed turn.
5. **Equipment sustain.** [Heart of Nizchetus](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Heart%20of%20Nizchetus) × 2 (unique, draw-3-pick-down-3 at unlock) smooths the combo. [Incriminating Videotape](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Incriminating%20Videotape) + [The Signet of King Saul](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=The%20Signet%20of%20King%20Saul) are movable via [Heidelberg Castle, Germany](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Heidelberg%20Castle%2C%20Germany) for double-dipped equip triggers.
6. **Defend during setup.** [Deflection](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Deflection) × 6 (DOM bounce), [Bait and Switch](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Bait%20and%20Switch) × 4, [Guard Dogs](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Guard%20Dogs) × 4 (retainer-provided intercept via [J. S. Simmons, Esq.](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=J.%20S.%20Simmons%2C%20Esq.) and [Tasha Morgan](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Tasha%20Morgan)), [Deep Ecology](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Deep%20Ecology) × 4 intercept, [Form of the Bat](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Form%20of%20the%20Bat) × 4 double-mode.
7. **Lunge.** Post-setup, each equipped vampire bleeds multiple times per turn through British Museum unlocks; Living Manse +1 bleed per copy; [Conditioning](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Conditioning) × 2 for un-bounce-proof bleed amp; [Eat the Rich](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Eat%20the%20Rich) × 2 (political, direct pool hit).
8. **The Darby dance — [NRA PAC](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=NRA%20PAC) × 1.** Event that unlocks a minion at end of turn. After spending British Museum's once-per-turn unlock for an attack sequence, NRA PAC re-readies the vampire for *defence* next turn — a signature Darby Keeney timing trick.

## Key disciplines and clans

- **Clan:** Tzimisce.
- **Disciplines:** `[DOM]` (Govern, Conditioning, Deflection), `[VIC]` (Living Manse is `[vic]` equipment-equipping, Obedient Flesh × 2 combat mod), `[PRO]` (Earth Meld, Form of Mist, Earth Control stealth), `[ANI]` (Deep Ecology, Form of the Bat inferior).
- **Sect:** Anarch — Baron voting, Eat the Rich fallback. The archetype itself is not as Anarch-gated as Platinum Revelation or Gangrel Thing; the Anarch sect primarily enables the crypt and Eat the Rich.

## Support / tech

### The engine
[The British Museum](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=The%20British%20Museum%2C%20London) × 5 (redundant copies because contested) → unlock on successful equip with non-electronic non-vehicle non-weapon equipment. Living Manse is VIC non-weapon equipment; equipping it triggers British Museum → unlocked vampire can now bleed at +1 thanks to Manse.

### Heidelberg double-dip
[Heidelberg Castle, Germany](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Heidelberg%20Castle%2C%20Germany) moves equipment between your ready vampires. Moving [Incriminating Videotape](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Incriminating%20Videotape) (per-bleed bonus) or [The Signet of King Saul](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=The%20Signet%20of%20King%20Saul) after the bleed lands triggers each card's ability on multiple vampires per turn.

### Darby dance (NRA PAC)
British Museum's once-per-turn unlock is spent on the attack; NRA PAC at end-of-turn re-unlocks the vampire so defence reactions (Deflection, Bait and Switch) are available next turn.

### Defence
- Deflection × 6 — DOM bounce, the primary defence.
- Bait and Switch × 4 — universal bounce.
- Guard Dogs × 4 + retainer (J. S. Simmons, Tasha Morgan) — retainer-provided intercept.
- Deep Ecology × 4 — +1 intercept ANI.
- Form of the Bat × 4 PRO intercept mode.

### Combat bail
Earth Meld × 4, Form of Mist × 4, Obedient Flesh × 2 (VIC dodge/escape), Living Manse burn-to-end-combat.

### Pool
Villein × 3, Wider View × 1, Eat the Rich × 2, Two Wrongs × 2 (bounce-like master).

## Variants

- **Darby Keeney's latest** — adds [Dominate Kine](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Dominate%20Kine) (DOM bleed bonus), equipment swaps toward blood-regen ([Cooler](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Cooler), [Palatial Estate](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Palatial%20Estate)), and [Powerbase: Savannah](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Powerbase%3A%20Savannah) as anti-rush defence [src-010].

## Weaknesses

1. **British Museum contested.** It's unique; opposing Methuselahs can contest. Five-copy redundancy mitigates but not perfectly.
2. **Combat with equipment burned.** When Manse is burned to end combat, the vampire loses +1 bleed until another Manse is equipped.
3. **Equipment-removal tech.** [Pentex™ Subversion](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Pentex%E2%84%A2%20Subversion), [Wash](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Wash), [Foreshadowing Destruction](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Foreshadowing%20Destruction) on Manse kills the combo.
4. **Setup-turn vulnerability.** The deck takes 3–4 turns to install the engine; fast powerbleed preys (Hesha's Emporium) can punch through before the combo comes online.
5. **Electronic equipment not eligible for British Museum.** Limits tech slots to non-electronic — no .44 Magnum tap, no Learjet chains.
