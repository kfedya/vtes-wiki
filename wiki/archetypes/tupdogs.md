# Tupdogs

Sabbat **rush** archetype. Uses [Tupdog](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/crypt/index.html?card=Tupdog) (cap 1 Gargoyle Sabbat Tremere-antitribu slave; unlimited copies since "Tupdogs are not unique" and "After the end of your minion phase, burn Tupdog") as disposable rush minions. Each Tupdog can enter combat with any minion as a directed action; the deck fills them with Potence/Visceratika/Animalism combat cards (Immortal Grapple × 10, Raking Talons × 9, Brick by Brick × 8, Disarm × 6, Roundhouse × 4) to send opposing vampires to torpor. Then [Graverobbing](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Graverobbing) × 8 steals the torpored vampires for pool damage. [Rock Cat](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Rock%20Cat) × 10 (cap-4 ally) adds combat ends and persistent threats. Codex Top Tier [src-019].

Canonical decklist: **Danilo Torrisi**, GP Milan Season 2024, 2023-12-09, TWDA id 10853 [src-019].

## Core idea

1. **Influence 2–3 Tremere antitribu first.** [Antonio d'Erlette](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/crypt/index.html?card=Antonio%20d%27Erlette), [Esoara](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/crypt/index.html?card=Esoara), [Janine](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/crypt/index.html?card=Janine), [Mosfair](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/crypt/index.html?card=Mosfair), [Ember Wright](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/crypt/index.html?card=Ember%20Wright), [Keith Moody](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/crypt/index.html?card=Keith%20Moody), [Saiz](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/crypt/index.html?card=Saiz) — all single-copy. **Tremere antitribu are required for Tupdog** because Tupdog is "Tremere antitribu slave."
2. **Influence Tupdogs cheaply.** Cap-1, 21 copies in crypt. Each Tupdog pays 1 blood; burned at end of your minion phase.
3. **Rush with combat pile.** Each Tupdog enters combat as a directed action with any minion (card text). Combat pile includes:
   - [Immortal Grapple](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Immortal%20Grapple) × 10 — POT lock close; prevent evasion.
   - [Raking Talons](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Raking%20Talons) × 9 — VIS aggravated strike.
   - [Brick by Brick](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Brick%20by%20Brick) × 8 — maneuver to close.
   - [Disarm](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Disarm) × 6 — strip equipment.
   - [Roundhouse](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Roundhouse) × 4 — POT +1 strength hand strike.
4. **Steal torpored vampires with [Graverobbing](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Graverobbing) × 8.** Sabbat-gated action to take an opposing torpored vampire for 2 pool on the prey.
5. **Rock Cat swarm.** [Rock Cat](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Rock%20Cat) × 10 — ally, cap-4 (3 life, 2 strength), ends combat on press. Recruitable even pre-Tremere-antitribu-in-play via [Autarkis Persecution](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Autarkis%20Persecution).
6. **Economy: [Autarkis Persecution](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Autarkis%20Persecution) × 8 + [Ashur Tablets](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Ashur%20Tablets) × 6.** Autarkis is a political action: 2 pool damage per non-Sabbat non-Anarch Methuselah — doubles as pool hit and pool gain (ruling-dependent; see forum references). Ashur Tablets × 6 rebuild engine.
7. **Defence.** [Deflection](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Deflection) × 6 bounce. [As the Crow](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=As%20the%20Crow) × 6 ANI action-mod for Rock Cat rush plays (bypass block restrictions). [Secure Haven](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Secure%20Haven) protects a Tremere antitribu host.

## Key disciplines and clans

- **Clan:** Gargoyle (Tupdog) + Tremere antitribu (hosts). Tupdog is Tremere antitribu slave (see [[entities/clans/gargoyle|gargoyle]], [[entities/clans/tremere-antitribu|tremere-antitribu]]).
- **Disciplines on Tupdog:** `[vis]` inferior — Visceratika costs Tupdog 1 less blood (Raking Talons superior is aggravated). Plays POT (Immortal Grapple, Roundhouse, Disarm) and VIS (Raking Talons, Brick by Brick) via clan/slave access.
- **Sect:** Sabbat — Graverobbing, Autarkis Persecution, and the deck's flavour.

## Support / tech

### Rush tempo
Tupdog is a cap-1, costs 1 blood to influence, burns at end of minion phase. Each turn: influence 1–2 Tupdogs → enter combat with a prey minion → send to torpor with Raking Talons aggravated + Brick by Brick maneuver + Immortal Grapple lock.

### Graverobbing cycle
Each prey vampire sent to torpor by a Tupdog becomes a Graverobbing target — steal the vampire for +2 pool damage.

### Pool cost
Tupdogs are 1 blood each; Rock Cat is 4 pool; Graverobbing is 1 pool. Autarkis Persecution × 8 recovers pool; Ashur Tablets × 6 rebuilds the combat pile.

### Rock Cat role
Cap-4 ally, 3 life, 2 strength, ends combat on press. Persistent (unlike Tupdog, doesn't burn end-of-turn). Graverobbing becomes a backup for Rock Cat since Rock Cats stick around.

## Variants

- **Nephandus version** — less aggressive, drops the rush in favour of Nephandus ally bleeds (see [[archetypes/nephandii|Nephandii]]).
- **Dominate version** — drop Rock Cat + Autarkis, add DOM bleed module (Conditioning + Govern + Deflection) for faster direct-oust.

## Weaknesses

1. **Archon Investigation.** Tupdog combat is technically the acting minion diablerie-bait; well-timed Archon burns the Tremere antitribu host when Antonio gets diablerised.
2. **Ally-hate tech.** Pentex on Rock Cat; Frontal Assault on Tupdog disables the combat pile.
3. **Combat-end tech.** Majesty / Ahriman's Demesne / Entombment counter by ending combat before Raking Talons lands.
4. **Fast oust predators.** Hesha's Emporium ousts before the Rock Cat / Tupdog chain matures.
5. **Tremere antitribu crypt dependence.** If no Tremere antitribu in play, Tupdog is stuck — the slave relationship is a hard requirement.
