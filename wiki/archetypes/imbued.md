# Imbued (Hunters) — archetype umbrella

Umbrella page for all Imbued-centred archetypes. Imbued are **not vampires** — they are mortal Hunters introduced in *Nights of Reckoning* (Hunters Hunted II). They ignore or side-step huge chunks of normal VTES gameplay:

- Immune to frenzy; unaffected by most vampire-specific trait cards (Fame-damage, blood-cost actions, diablerie).
- No blood pool; they spend **convictions** (a separate resource). Each Imbued enters play with 2 convictions; more are harvested via creed abilities or Master cards.
- No clan — instead, a **creed** (Avenger, Defender, Innocent, Judge, Martyr, Redeemer, Visionary).
- No standard Disciplines — instead, creed-linked **virtues** (Defense `[def]`, Innocence `[inn]`, Judgment `[jud]`, Martyrdom `[mar]`, Redemption `[red]`, Vengeance `[ven]`, Vision `[viz]`).
- Built-in intercept against vampires (printed card text on reactions like [Discern](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Discern)).

Imbued are a TWDA fringe but have posted real tournament finishes — including a **54-player Atlantic Cup 2GW8.5+5** (Bill Troxel, 2022) and an outright win at a 17-player Asian continental (Lucinio de Guzman Jr., ACC Manila 2018). This page catalogues the four repeatable archetypes plus two fringe builds.

## Shared toolbox

Cards that appear in almost every competitive Imbued deck:

- **[Angel of Berlin](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Angel%20of%20Berlin)** (Master: out-of-turn) — unlock an Imbued OR equip from hand. 3–5 copies standard. Tempo + defensive equip-in-response.
- **[Fortschritt Library](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Fortschritt%20Library)** (Master, unique) — tutor a Gehenna event to hand. 1–2 copies in event-heavy builds.
- **[The Church of Vindicated Faith](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=The%20Church%20of%20Vindicated%20Faith)** (Master: unique location) — successful Imbued action moves 1 blood from bank to uncontrolled Imbued (influence-ramp).
- **[The Unmasking](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=The%20Unmasking)** (Gehenna event) — allies get +1 intercept vs. vampires. Near-universal include.
- **[Second Sight](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Second%20Sight)** (Conviction) — burn for +1 intercept or -1 intercept against. 6+ copies standard.
- **[React with Conviction](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=React%20with%20Conviction)** (Conviction) — cancel DOM/PRE/DEM/CHI/SER strike or action-card directed at Imbued, OR cancel a control-change effect. 4–8 copies.
- **[Strike with Conviction](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Strike%20with%20Conviction)** (Conviction) — aggravated hand/weapon strike +1 dmg, OR +1 bleed directed action. 3–7 copies.
- **[Discern](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Discern)** (Judgment Power reaction) — +1 intercept vs. a monster acting. Cheap intercept before convictions are spent.
- **[Vigilance](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Vigilance)** (Judgment Power) — unlock-after-successful-action OR 1-conviction unlock from locked. The Judge's Freak Drive.
- **[Determine](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Determine)** (Vision Reaction) — lock-and-redirect predator's bleed to predator's predator, OR cancel monster's action card. Dual-mode Imbued "Deflection".
- **[Rejuvenate](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Rejuvenate)** (Defense Power) — unlock-phase auto-heal 1 life OR 1-conviction action to add 1 blood/life to another minion.
- **[Champion](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Champion)** (Defense Power reaction, 2 conviction) — redirect a directed action targeting any Imbued into combat with this Imbued.
- **[Aranthebes, The Immortal](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Aranthebes%2C%20The%20Immortal)** (Unique action) — anti-predator tech: predator minions −1 stealth; cap ≤ 4 vampires −1 bleed vs. you.
- **Equipment base** — [Crusader Sword](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=The%20Crusader%20Sword), [Ivory Bow](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Ivory%20Bow), [Heart of Nizchetus](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Heart%20of%20Nizchetus), [Sport Bike](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Sport%20Bike). Angel of Berlin plants them on a ready Imbued out-of-turn.

## Archetype 1 — Emerald Legionnaire Swarm (Harbinger × Imbued hybrid) ⭐⭐⭐

**Strongest Imbued finish in TWDA history.** The engine is not pure Imbued — it's a hybrid: half Imbued wall, half Harbinger of Skulls crypt powering an ally-recursion loop.

### Reference decks
- **"Dumpster Fire of Pure Hate"** — Bill Troxel, Atlantic Cup 2022-05-21 (**54-player 3R+F, 2GW8.5+5**). TWDA id [10044](https://vdb.im/twd/10044). Troxel's comment: *"I have virtually constructed an Emerald Legionnaire deck for every clan, plus Imbued; this is the Imbued deck. Stay alive with Liquidations and defend with Imbued until you can amass an army of ELs."*
- **"The New Inquisition"** — Luca Turicchi, Secure Haven Castelnuovo di Garfagnana 2023-11-26 (18-player 2R+F). TWDA id [11049](https://vdb.im/twd/11049).

### Engine

- **Crypt split** — 4× [Mina Grotius](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/crypt/index.html?card=Mina%20Grotius) (Harbinger of Skulls unique vampire) + Imbued Hunters (Jennie "Cassie247" Orne × 3–4, Jack "Hannibal137" Harmon × 2, Marion "Teacher193" Perks, François "Warden" Loehr). Turicchi's variant swaps Mina for Babalawo Alafin + Solomon Batanea (other unique Harbingers).
- **[Emerald Legionnaire](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Emerald%20Legionnaire) × 10** (Wraith ally, 2 life / 2 strength / 1 bleed). Printed ability: *"If you control fewer copies of Emerald Legionnaire than ready unique Harbinger of Skulls, you can remove 7 cards in your ash heap from the game to move an Emerald Legionnaire from ash heap to ready region with 2 life."* → With 1 ready Mina in play, you can resurrect 1 EL per unlock phase.
- **[Liquidation](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Liquidation) × 9** (Master, unique-each) — burn 7 cards from library top to ash → gain 3 pool. Double-duty: **feeds the 7-card exile cost** of EL resurrection AND bloats 3 pool per copy.
- **[Unity](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Unity) × 3–4** (Master, unique hunter-list) — lock + 1 pool to move 2 conviction/creed/virtue cards from ash to top of library. Recycles spent convictions back into the engine.
- **[The Parthenon](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=The%20Parthenon) × 4** + [Wider View](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Wider%20View) × 4 — Master-phase tempo / card filter.
- **Combat / intercept on ELs** — [Breath of Thanatos](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Breath%20of%20Thanatos) × 5 (action-mod/combat dual), [Vigilance](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Vigilance) × 4, [Champion](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Champion) × 1–2.

**Tempo:** Slow control → mid-game swarm. Defends for 4–5 turns with Imbued wall while Liquidation bloats and ELs accumulate in ash; from turn 6 onwards, resurrected EL pack grinds prey with buff-equipped bleeds.

## Archetype 2 — Gehenna Cascade / Wormwood ⭐⭐

### Reference deck
**"Wormwood"** — Nuno Moure, Elysium: Almada, Portugal, 2025-05-31 (12-player 2R+F). TWDA id [12361](https://vdb.im/twd/12361) [src-033]. Raw JSON mirrored at `raw/decks/033-wormwood.json`.

### Engine

- Pure Imbued crypt: 12 G4 Imbued, mixed creeds.
- 14 Gehenna events (Wormwood × 3 + The Unmasking × 2 + 9 uniques: Blood Trade, Blood Weakens, The New Inquisition, Nightmares upon Nightmares, Recalled to the Founder, Restricted Vitae, The Slow Withering, Thirst, Torpid Blood).
- [Wormwood](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Wormwood) enters with 10 counters; each Gehenna event played anywhere at the table burns 1. While X counters remain, every vampire of capacity > X is capped at X (min 1). Bloat/ramp decks collapse when cap shrinks to 3–4 by turn 6–8.
- Finisher: block → [Weighted Walking Stick](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Weighted%20Walking%20Stick) × 4 (str+1 melee, 5 counters) + [Target Vitals](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Target%20Vitals) × 4 (aim +2 dmg + pool loss to controller) + [Strike with Conviction](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Strike%20with%20Conviction) aggravated rider. 3–5 pool damage per block.
- Event insurance: [Not to Be](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Not%20to%20Be) cancels an opponent's disruptive event.
- Anti-predator: Aranthebes + [Millicent Smith (Master)](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Millicent%20Smith%2C%20Puritan%20Vampire%20Hunter) (migrates to predator; if predator's vamp is blocked, burn both without combat).

**Tempo:** Control/attrition. Needs 5–6 Gehenna plays before the capacity cap hurts big-vampire decks.

## Archetype 3 — Horsemen of Gehenna (Judge-rush with guns) ⭐⭐

### Reference deck
**"Horsemen of Gehenna"** — Lucinio de Guzman Jr., ACC Manila 2018-10-20 (17-player 3R+F, **2GW3+4 — won the event**). TWDA id [2018accmp](https://vdb.im/twd/2018accmp).

### Engine

- **12-Imbued Judge-pure crypt:** Jennie "Cassie247" Orne × 3, Travis "Traveler72" Miller × 3, Jack "Hannibal137" Harmon × 3, François "Warden" Loehr × 3. All have `[def]` or `[jud]`.
- **[Lock](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Lock) × 6** (Defense action) — puts a card on a minion: *"other Methuselahs' actions targeting this minion cost monsters an additional blood or life; if this minion is a monster, he or she burns a blood or life when attempting an action or a block."* — paralysing tax on opposing vampires.
- **[Suppressing Fire](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Suppressing%20Fire) × 4** (Action Modifier) — ready unlocked minion with a gun gives blocker −1 intercept. Enables ranged-bleed lunge.
- **Weapon pile** — Desert Eagle × 2, Sniper Rifle, .44 Magnum. Allows ranged strikes + Suppressing Fire backup.
- **9-event Gehenna subplot** — Wormwood + Dragonbound + Torpid Blood + Restricted Vitae + Recalled to the Founder + The Slow Withering + Thirst + The Unmasking + Dr. Marisa Fletcher, CDC. Half-scale Wormwood-style pressure.
- **Defense base** — React with Conviction × 4, Second Sight × 4, Vigilance × 3, Champion × 2, Rejuvenate × 3, Discern × 2.

**Tempo:** Medium-rush. Lock + Suppressing Fire forces prey to ration actions; weapon strikes finish. Only Imbued deck that has outright **won** a multi-round event.

## Archetype 4 — Friendly Imbued (classic Target Vitals wall) ⭐⭐

### Reference deck
**"Friendly Imbued"** — Gabriel Ron, Sons of Fangs Barcelona 2019-01-20 (20-player 2R+F, 1GW4+2). TWDA id [2019sofbsp](https://vdb.im/twd/2019sofbsp).

### Engine

- **Mixed-creed crypt**: Jennie × 3, Travis × 3, Jack × 2, John "Cop90" O'Malley × 2, Sixofswords29, Warden.
- **Combat rider** — [Target Vitals](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Target%20Vitals) × 6 + Dodge × 2. Each successful block → 2+ pool damage to prey.
- **Weapon arsenal** — Crusader Sword, Sniper Rifle, Saturday-Night Special, Flash Grenade × 2, Sport Bike. Tutored via Angel of Berlin × 5.
- **Ally support** — Ossian + Gregory Winter + Carlton Van Wyk + Vagabond Mystic (pentex-safe bench).
- **Event sub-plot** — NRA PAC + The Unmasking × 2. Not a Gehenna-cascade deck — NRA PAC doubles Desert Eagle / Sniper Rifle chain.
- **Anti-event insurance** — Not to Be + Archon Investigation.
- **Standard conviction base** — React with Conviction × 4, Second Sight × 5, Strike with Conviction × 6, Determine × 3, Champion × 2, Vigilance × 3.

**Tempo:** Control/wall. Template build — closest to the "default" Imbued deck; the ancestor Wormwood branched from.

## Fringe builds

### Karen Suadela hybrid — "Suadela is driving to Murcia"
**Albert Dulcet Belmonte, Powerbase Badalona 2020-01-19 (24-player 2R+F, 1GW3.5+1.5).** TWDA id [2020pbbs](https://vdb.im/twd/2020pbbs).

Crypt is **Karen Suadela × 4** (G5 Toreador, [obf][pre][CEL][POT], titled Camarilla Prince of Houston — *"While Karen is a prince, Gehenna events do not affect her"*) + 8 Imbued. Plan: Imbued wall sets up, then play **Judgment: Camarilla Segregation × 5** (Imbued action: makes target vampire Camarilla; hand-off to Karen) → **Fall of the Camarilla** for pool-damage swings. Karen is uniquely event-immune while Prince, letting the Imbued side stack Gehenna events without hurting her. 9× Ashur Tablets + Liquidation × 3 + Jake Washington × 2 for bloat. Dulcet's note: *"Start building a wall with the Imbued and then play Karen."*

### Dance Imbued / Talbot's Chainsaw — "Balls of Stihl"
**Karl Schaefer, Origins Friday 1 2021-10-01 (10-player 2R+F, 0GW1.5+2.5).** TWDA id [9848](https://vdb.im/twd/9848).

Equipment-brawler fringe: **[Talbot's Chainsaw](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Talbot%27s%20Chainsaw) × 2** (unique weapon, strike 3 damage; if bearer ready at your unlock, 3 unpreventable self-damage; +1 stealth directed action to enter combat; mandatory press). Paired with [Rejuvenate](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Rejuvenate) × 3 self-heal + massive conviction pile (React × 8, Second Sight × 7, Strike × 7). Angel of Berlin × 5 juggles equipment between Imbued. Weaker finish, but an interesting equipment-tempo experiment.

| Archetype | Dominant creeds | Signature |
|---|---|---|
| EL Swarm | Visionary / Judge (+ Harbinger vampires) | Emerald Legionnaire resurrection loop |
| Wormwood cascade | Mixed (all 7 creeds) | Gehenna event capacity-decay |
| Horsemen of Gehenna | Judge / Defender (pure) | Lock + Suppressing Fire + guns |
| Friendly Imbued | Mixed (Visionary heavy) | Target Vitals combat rider |
| Suadela hybrid | Judge (for Segregation action) | Judgment: Camarilla Segregation |
| Dance Imbued | Defender (for Rejuvenate) | Talbot's Chainsaw juggling |

## Weaknesses (common to all Imbued)

1. **Ally hate** — Pentex™ Subversion, Anarch Troublemaker, Ward: Imbued hit hard. Imbued builds skimp on Pentex counter-tech.
2. **Rush pressure before setup** — Imbued are medium-pace; 4–5 turns to assemble walls/ELs/Gehenna stack. A turn-2 rush (Tupdogs, Brujah Line Brawl) can torpor key Imbued before convictions stabilise.
3. **Stealth-powerbleed preys** — React with Conviction only cancels DOM/PRE/DEM/CHI/SER effects; OBF stealth (Malk' 22, Ravnos Bonds) punches through.
4. **Weenie/small-cap decks** — Wormwood cap-decay does nothing against decks already running cap-1 to cap-3 crypt.
5. **Event counters** — Not to Be / Delay the Will of the Dead cancel a pivotal Gehenna event play.
6. **Intra-Imbued mirror** — Two Imbued tables play very slowly; whoever gets Angel of Berlin first wins.
