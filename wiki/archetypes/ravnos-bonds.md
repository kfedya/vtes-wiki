# Ravnos Bonds

Ravnos **stealth-and-bleed** archetype built on [Break the Bonds](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Break%20the%20Bonds) × 16 — a `[CHI]` action that bleeds + fetches another Ravnos into play on success. Each successful Bonds bleed both deals pool damage and grows the table, creating an "unstoppable wave" of bleeds that accelerates exponentially. Paired with [Memory Rift](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Memory%20Rift) × 10 as the primary OBF stealth source (doesn't clog hand) and [Visions of Gehenna](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Visions%20of%20Gehenna) × 8 +bleed amp. Codex Top Tier [src-016].

Canonical decklist: **Simone Parmeggiani**, EC 2024 Day 1, 2024-09-28, TWDA id 11668 [src-016].

## Core idea

1. **Influence one Ravnos (any).** Pool of G6 Ravnos: [Sreelekha](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/crypt/index.html?card=Sreelekha) × 2, [Roberto Rivamonte](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/crypt/index.html?card=Roberto%20Rivamonte) × 2, [Gathii](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/crypt/index.html?card=Gathii) × 2, plus singletons [Doc Martina](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/crypt/index.html?card=Doc%20Martina), [Luciano Carvalho](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/crypt/index.html?card=Luciano%20Carvalho), [Phaibun](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/crypt/index.html?card=Phaibun), [Jean-François](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/crypt/index.html?card=Jean-Fran%C3%A7ois), [Zafira](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/crypt/index.html?card=Zafira), [Oleg Kaprizov](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/crypt/index.html?card=Oleg%20Kaprizov).
2. **Bleed with [Break the Bonds](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Break%20the%20Bonds) × 16.** CHI action — bleed. On success, put a Ravnos from your hand into play locked. The deck snowballs: each successful Bonds is 1 bleed *and* 1 new Ravnos for next turn's bleed chain.
3. **Stealth with [Memory Rift](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Memory%20Rift) × 10** — OBF/AUS double mode (+1 stealth, +1 intercept reaction mode) that draws a replacement card. Doesn't clog hand. Plus [Lost in Crowds](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Lost%20in%20Crowds) × 3, [Faceless Night](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Faceless%20Night) × 3, [Elder Impersonation](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Elder%20Impersonation) × 4, [Spying Mission](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Spying%20Mission) × 4, [True Love's Face](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=True%20Love%27s%20Face) × 2, [Veil the Legions](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Veil%20the%20Legions) × 3.
4. **Bleed amp.** [Visions of Gehenna](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Visions%20of%20Gehenna) × 8 (CHI — +1 bleed + burn 1 blood), [Power of One](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Power%20of%20One) × 4 (POT stealth+bleed; splash for some Ravnos with POT), [Public Trust](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Public%20Trust) × 4 (PRE +1 bleed).
5. **Wave finisher.** [Week of Nightmares](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Week%20of%20Nightmares) × 2 + [Club Illusion](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Club%20Illusion) (Chimerstry stealth location) create the decisive wave when 3+ Ravnos are ready.
6. **Economy.** [Dabbler](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Dabbler) × 4 (unlock / blood after any triple-discipline action — deck runs CHI/OBF/PRE so triggers reliably); [Effective Management](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Effective%20Management) × 3 (Master: draw 3 discard 3).
7. **Defence.** [Visions of Zapathasura](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Visions%20of%20Zapathasura) × 5 is the *only* bleed-defence (CHI reaction: cancel an incoming bleed via illusion), [Delaying Tactics](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Delaying%20Tactics) × 2 anti-vote. **No combat cards.** Event: [Narrow Minds](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Narrow%20Minds) cancels opposing action-mods mid-action.
8. **Anti-master / anti-intercept.** [Wash](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Wash) × 2 burns the prey's permanent intercept locations (Club Illusion, Creepshow Casino) or Minion Tap / Vessel.

## Key disciplines and clans

- **Clan:** Ravnos.
- **Disciplines:** `[CHI]` (Break the Bonds, Visions of Gehenna, Visions of Zapathasura, Week of Nightmares), `[OBF]` (Memory Rift, Lost in Crowds, Faceless Night, Elder Impersonation, Spying Mission, True Love's Face), `[PRE]` (Public Trust), `[POT]` (Power of One), `[ANI]` (Ravnos V5 signature — inferior on most). Triple-discipline density feeds Dabbler.
- **Sect:** Anarch (most G6 Ravnos are Anarch-aligned in V5; Anarch Railroad in the deck).

## Support / tech

### The Break the Bonds engine
With 16 copies (18% of library), Break the Bonds *is* the deck. Every action is Bonds. Stealth modifiers (31 cards) ensure it lands. Each landing = +1 Ravnos in play.

### Dabbler triggers
The deck plays 3 disciplines per action regularly (CHI bleed + OBF stealth + PRE bleed amp or POT stealth amp); each Dabbler in play unlocks or refunds 1 blood per turn.

### No bounce, no combat
Visions of Zapathasura × 5 is the entirety of bleed-defence — relies on prey's predator / prey bouncing their own bleeds. No combat cards at all.

### Field Training splash
[Field Training](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Field%20Training) × 3 gives OBF to a Ravnos that doesn't have it, widening the stealth pool.

## Variants

- **Guard Dogs splash** — for an extra unlock/intercept mode.
- **Bleed-accelerated** — drop Visions of Zapathasura for more Visions of Gehenna / bleed amp.

## Weaknesses

1. **Zero combat.** Rush decks send Ravnos to torpor, freezing the Bonds chain.
2. **Heavy intercept walls** burn through the +1/+2 stealth stack when multiple copies land on a single action.
3. **Pool vulnerability.** No Villein, no bounce — a predator's bleed lands directly and Visions of Zapathasura has to be drawn on time.
4. **Single-axis strategy.** The deck cannot pivot. If Break the Bonds is Wash'd out or the crypt pool is exhausted, the engine stalls.
5. **Anti-CHI tech.** Chimerstry is a narrow discipline; prey running [Direct Intervention](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Direct%20Intervention) (they do, there's 1 copy in your own deck) can cancel a key Visions of Gehenna or Break the Bonds.
