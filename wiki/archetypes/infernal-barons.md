# Infernal Barons

Anarch Infernal **vote** archetype headlined by **[Arishat](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/crypt/index.html?card=Arishat)** (Baali Baron) alongside [Xeper, Sultan of Lepers](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/crypt/index.html?card=Xeper%2C%20Sultan%20of%20Lepers) (Baali; +1 vote per ready unique infernal). Arishat's ability + [Condemnation: Mute](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Condemnation%3A%20Mute) × 5 control opponent voting power; the deck then smashes through with [Kine Resources Contested](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Kine%20Resources%20Contested) × 11, [Reckless Agitation](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Reckless%20Agitation) × 3, [Eat the Rich](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Eat%20the%20Rich) × 3. Codex Runner-up [src-023].

Canonical decklist: **Ke Carlton**, AUSNZ Continental 2022, 2021-11-06, TWDA id 10276 [src-023].

## Core idea

1. **Seat [Arishat](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/crypt/index.html?card=Arishat) × 4 (Baali Anarch Baron).** Support crypt: Xeper × 2 (Baali), Vivian VI × 2 (Ministry — from Platinum Revelation shell), Elisha Tucker × 2, Jacques Rouge, Atiena.
2. **Control opposing votes.** [Condemnation: Mute](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Condemnation%3A%20Mute) × 5 — DAI action to strip a minion's vote capacity.
3. **Referendum chain.** Kine Resources Contested × 11 (!) + Reckless Agitation × 3 + Eat the Rich × 3.
4. **Vote amp.** [Voter Captivation](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Voter%20Captivation) × 4, [Bewitching Oration](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Bewitching%20Oration) × 2, [Monkey Wrench](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Monkey%20Wrench) × 2 (opportunistic bleed amp).
5. **Stealth.** [Forgotten Labyrinth](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Forgotten%20Labyrinth) × 5 (bypasses Second Tradition: Domain), [Lost in Crowds](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Lost%20in%20Crowds) × 5, [Faceless Night](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Faceless%20Night) × 2.
6. **Bloat.** [Villein](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Villein) × 6, [Information Highway](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Information%20Highway) × 3, [Wider View](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Wider%20View) × 2, [Garibaldi-Meucci Museum](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Garibaldi-Meucci%20Museum) × 2 (fetch bounces or referenda).
7. **Defence.** [Bait and Switch](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Bait%20and%20Switch) × 4, [Organized Resistance](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Organized%20Resistance) × 2, [Majesty](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Majesty) × 4 combat-end.
8. **Utility.** [Unleash Hell's Fury](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Unleash%20Hell%27s%20Fury) × 3 defensive/disruption.

## Key disciplines and clans

- **Clan:** Baali (Arishat, Xeper, Jacques Rouge) + Ministry (Vivian VI) + splashes.
- **Disciplines:** `[DAI]` (Condemnation: Mute, Unleash Hell's Fury), `[PRE]` (Voter Captivation, Bewitching Oration), `[OBF]` (Forgotten Labyrinth, Lost in Crowds, Faceless Night).
- **Sect:** Anarch — Arishat is Baron; Organized Resistance and Anarch Railroad Anarch-gated. Baali Infernal trait = -1 pool/unlock per infernal.

## Variants

- **Without Xeper** — substitute Entrancement or more Infernal utility.
- **Dominica variant** — Dominica crypt instead of Arishat for contest resistance + rush defence.

## Weaknesses

1. **Infernal pool tax.** 1 pool per unlock phase per infernal = -3 to -5 per turn; Villein × 6 compensates.
2. **Anti-vote tech.** Delaying Tactics (Arishat doesn't bypass it natively), Parity Shift, Scourge.
3. **No printed bounce beyond Bait and Switch × 4.** Heavy predator pressure drains.
4. **Contest risk on Arishat.** Unique high-cap vampire — another Baali player or contest from same clan slows the plan.
