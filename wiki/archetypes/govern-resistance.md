# Govern Resistance

Tzimisce **Anarch Baron toolbox** named for the two load-bearing cards: **[Govern the Unaligned](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Govern%20the%20Unaligned) × 12** (Dominate action — influence cheaper at inferior, bleed at superior) and **[Organized Resistance](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Organized%20Resistance) × 7** (Anarch intercept). Influences the oldest Tzimisce Baron first, rides Govern to discount the rest of the crypt into play, then flexes between bleed (Dominate) and rush (Deep Song) while tanking combat with Protean's bail tech. Codex lists it among the current-meta Top Tier (non-Top-Dog) [src-009].

Canonical decklist: **Bill Troxel**, NAC 2024, 2024-06-22, TWDA id 11496 [src-009].

## Core idea

1. **Influence oldest first.** The Tzimisce crypt concentrates in the mid caps; [Adrino Manauara](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/crypt/index.html?card=Adrino%20Manauara) × 4 and [Ángel Guerrero](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/crypt/index.html?card=%C3%81ngel%20Guerrero) × 3 are the backbone. Seat the oldest available, then use superior [Govern the Unaligned](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Govern%20the%20Unaligned) (influence target vampire of capacity 3 less than the acting vampire for 2 blood transferred + bleed side effect) to accelerate the rest into play.
2. **Bleed via Govern + [Conditioning](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Conditioning).** Each Govern bleed stacks with Conditioning × 4 (DOM +1 bleed, unpreventable by stealth/bounce modifiers printed on non-DOM/PRE reactions) for a 3–4 bleed baseline.
3. **Add stealth with Protean.** [Earth Control](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Earth%20Control) × 4 and [Form of Mist](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Form%20of%20Mist) × 7 (dual stealth/combat-bail); occasional [Form of the Bat](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Form%20of%20the%20Bat) × 3.
4. **Rush / lock with [Deep Song](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Deep%20Song) × 3.** ANI action: enter combat with an opposing minion. Three uses: rush a prey threat, lock a defender (then Earth Meld unlock + bleed), or bleed directly with vampires that don't have Dominate. Deep Song's value compounds with the wide intercept wall.
5. **Wall with intercept + bounce.** [Organized Resistance](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Organized%20Resistance) × 7 (Anarch +1 intercept), [Deep Ecology](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Deep%20Ecology) × 4, [Form of the Bat](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Form%20of%20the%20Bat) × 3 intercept mode, [Deflection](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Deflection) × 4 (DOM bounce), [Bait and Switch](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Bait%20and%20Switch) × 4 (any-discipline bounce), [Delaying Tactics](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Delaying%20Tactics) × 3 (anti-vote).
6. **Bail combat with Protean.** [Earth Meld](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Earth%20Meld) × 9 + [Form of Mist](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Form%20of%20Mist) × 7 = 16-card combat module; [Donnybrook](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Donnybrook) × 1 is the lone disruption tool for aggravated damage.
7. **Bloat and pool pressure.** [Villein](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Villein) × 2 and [Wider View](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Wider%20View) × 2 pool-recovery; [Eat the Rich](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Eat%20the%20Rich) × 2 political pool hit; [Channel 10](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Channel%2010) unique tap engine.

## Key disciplines and clans

- **Clan:** Tzimisce (ANI/DOM/VIC signature), with 1 [Kuyén](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/crypt/index.html?card=Kuy%C3%A9n) Gangrel splash (ANI/PRO overlap).
- **Disciplines:** `[DOM]` (Govern, Conditioning, Deflection), `[PRO]` (Earth Meld, Form of Mist, Earth Control), `[ANI]` (Deep Ecology, Deep Song, Form of the Bat), `[AUS]` inferior for Form of the Bat mode.
- **Sect:** Anarch — Organized Resistance, Anarch Railroad, Powerbase: Montreal, Baron voting all Anarch-gated.

## Variants

- **Claws variant** — aggressive combat via more Deep Song + PRO modifiers ([Claws of the Dead](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Claws%20of%20the%20Dead), [Donnybrook](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Donnybrook)).
- **Tap 'n' Bleed** — more Deep Song to lock defenders; swap stealth for disruption cards like [Invigorate](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Invigorate).
- **Anti-rush inclusions** — [Scourge of the Enochians](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Scourge%20of%20the%20Enochians) event vs swarms; [Groundfighting](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Groundfighting) to counter [[rulings/combat#immortal-grapple|Immortal Grapple]] lock.

Codex notes the archetype is close cousin to [[archetypes/gangrel-thing|Gangrel Thing]], differing mainly in Dominate focus vs Gangrel's naked-bleed payload [src-009].

## Weaknesses

1. **Dominate-bounce stack is mirror-exposed.** DOM bleeds bounce into DOM-based Deflections from DOM preys — the deck gets re-sent bleeds without bounce insurance beyond Bait and Switch × 4.
2. **Influence-curve dependency.** The deck assumes successful oldest-first influence; a bad opening crypt (all Adrinos) slows Govern's cost-savings loop.
3. **Thin pool recovery.** Villein × 2 + Wider View × 2 + Eat the Rich × 2 is modest. Against fast predators (Hesha Emporium, Salubri Powerbleed) the pool line can collapse before the Govern chain matures.
4. **Combat-ender decks.** No combat-ender cancel beyond Bait and Switch bounce. [Rötschreck](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=R%C3%B6tschreck) or [Entombment](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Entombment) from a combat-focused prey sends minions to torpor regardless of Earth Meld.
