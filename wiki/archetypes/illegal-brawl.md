# Illegal Brawl

Brujah **Anarch Baron toolbox** built around the double-threat action [Illegalism](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Illegalism) and the 3-way-combat action [Line Brawl](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Line%20Brawl). The deck swings between **rush** (brutal POT combat) and **bleed-at-stealth** (Power of One) turn by turn, decided by whichever move punishes the current predator/prey pair hardest. Codex classifies it as one of 4 ⭐ "Top Dog" current-meta archetypes [src-007].

Canonical decklist: **Darby Keeney**, Origins Thursday 2, 2024-06-20, TWDA id 11440 [src-007].

## Core idea

1. **Seat a Brujah Baron.** [Theo Bell (G6)](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/crypt/index.html?card=Theo%20Bell) × 2 (cap 9, Anarch Baron, `CEL/POT/PRE`) anchors the crypt; [Saku Pihlajamäki](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/crypt/index.html?card=Saku%20Pihlajam%C3%A4ki) × 2 and [Aline Gädeke](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/crypt/index.html?card=Aline%20G%C3%A4deke) × 2 fill cap-4/5 slots. Baron status powers the Anarch-gated payload (Illegalism, Organized Resistance, Powerbase: Los Angeles, An Anarch Manifesto).
2. **Drop [New Carthage](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=New%20Carthage) × 2.** Each copy doubles the bleed *and* vote power of a Baron — turn every naked bleed into a 2-bleed, every Baron vote into 4 votes.
3. **Flex [Illegalism](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Illegalism) × 9 and [Line Brawl](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Line%20Brawl) × 10.** Illegalism is an Anarch double-threat: at inferior it's a bleed mode, at superior (with [THA]/[CHI]) it adds stealth or stealth+1 bleed; Line Brawl is a `[POT]` 3-way-combat action that pulls another minion into the fight so the rush doesn't stall on dodge tech.
4. **Win combats with the classic POT package.** [Immortal Grapple](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Immortal%20Grapple) × 6 locks range close; [Dust Up](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Dust%20Up) × 9 adds reach strikes / cancels; [Diversion](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Diversion) × 4, [Bollix](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Bollix) × 2, [Disarm](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Disarm) × 2 handle opposing strikes or equipment; [Target Vitals](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Target%20Vitals) × 5 turns a 1-damage punch into unpreventable 3-damage; [Thrown Gate](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Thrown%20Gate) × 4 ends combat after damage lands so the vampire can keep acting; [Pulled Fangs](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Pulled%20Fangs) × 2 is a soft combat-ender for swing games.
5. **Mix stealth into bleeds.** [Power of One](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Power%20of%20One) × 6 is a POT bleed-stealth+1 mod that plays during an Illegalism bleed; stacked with New Carthage's double-bleed it lands a 4–5 bleed-at-stealth per attempt.
6. **Bloat to outpace the oust race.** [Villein](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Villein) × 5, [Blood Doll](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Blood%20Doll) × 2, [Papillon](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Papillon), [Carfax Abbey](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Carfax%20Abbey), [Taste of Vitae](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Taste%20of%20Vitae) × 4 cycle blood efficiently; [An Anarch Manifesto](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=An%20Anarch%20Manifesto) is almost-free with Dust Up's discard interaction (per Codex: *"the downside is almost negligible thanks to Dust Up"*).
7. **Defend without bounce bloat.** [Bait and Switch](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Bait%20and%20Switch) × 6 is the single bounce line; [Organized Resistance](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Organized%20Resistance) × 2 is the thin intercept (single-copy blocker relies on combat rebuttals rather than pre-block cancel).

## Key disciplines and clans

- **Clan:** Brujah — 12/12 crypt slots (some G5/G6 Brujah Anarch-faction).
- **Disciplines:** `[POT]` (Immortal Grapple, Dust Up, Target Vitals, Power of One, Disarm, Bollix, Thrown Gate); `[PRE]` (Bait and Switch mode); `[CEL]` (backup combat mode, Pulled Fangs). Line Brawl + Illegalism sit in the Anarch sect layer rather than a discipline.
- **Sect:** Anarch — Illegalism, Organized Resistance, Powerbase: Los Angeles, Baron vote totals, and the Anarch Manifesto all depend on the Anarch sect. See [[entities/sects/anarch|anarch]].

## Support / tech

### Combat (38 cards — 42% of library)
The combat pile is designed to punish *any* block: Immortal Grapple → close range → Dust Up / Target Vitals / Pulled Fangs / Thrown Gate. Diversion cancels the opposing strike or redirects it. Bollix cancels a strike card pre-resolution. Disarm strips equipment. Taste of Vitae × 4 recycles blood after each successful hit.

### Rush / 3-way
Line Brawl × 10 is a POT action that enters combat with an opposing minion, then adds a second opposing minion to the combat (3-way). Pair with Open War × 1 for a mass-combat finisher.

### Bleed / stealth
Illegalism × 9 (Anarch double-threat: bleed / bleed-at-stealth / rush on the same card name) + Power of One × 6 (POT stealth+1 bleed mod) + New Carthage × 2 (Baron double-bleed/vote).

### Bloat / cycle
Villein × 5, Blood Doll × 2, Taste of Vitae × 4, Papillon, Carfax Abbey, Dreams of the Sphinx × 2 (library smoothing + Anarch tutor via Powerbase: Los Angeles).

## Variants

- **Resistance** — max [Organized Resistance](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Organized%20Resistance), add [Constant Revolution](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Constant%20Revolution), [Stolen Police Cruiser](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Stolen%20Police%20Cruiser).
- **Chimerstry splash** — adds stealth to Illegalism and enables [Dabbler](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Dabbler).
- **Primogen** — [Protected District](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Protected%20District) + lower-capacity Brujah lean harder on the bleed plan.
- **Classic POT swap** — [Preternatural Strength](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Preternatural%20Strength) replaces Dust Up / Target Vitals for cheaper-but-preventable beats.

## Weaknesses

1. **Thin bounce.** Bait and Switch × 6 is the only defensive line. Wide vote decks that swing multiple terms per referendum (Mistress, Stanislava) punch through when Bait and Switch runs out.
2. **Single-copy Organized Resistance.** Only 2 copies; intercept is not the plan. Wall preys tank the rush and stall.
3. **Combat-heavy hand.** 38/90 combat cards means defensive blocks often draw non-block-relevant cards — Bait and Switch has to arrive on time or stealth bleeds land raw.
4. **Reliance on Dust Up for Manifesto bloat.** If Dust Up draws don't line up with Manifesto plays, the pool plan slips.
5. **Table combat tax.** 3-way Line Brawl aggroes neighbours that weren't the original target, occasionally building table alliances against you.
