# Baron Debate

Brujah **Anarch Baron wall** built around **[Brujah Debate](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Brujah%20Debate) × 8** — a Master-locking card that stacks on a single Anarch Baron and converts each stacked copy into strength, maneuvers, and other bonuses. Aline Gädeke's ability makes her the ideal Debate "victim" (highest capacity → biggest benefits). Organized Resistance × 18 is the densest intercept across any archetype in this batch. Codex New Kid [src-026].

Canonical decklist: **Jorge Delgado**, Week of Nightmares 2024 Day 1, 2024-09-24, TWDA id 11663 [src-026].

## Core idea

1. **Seat 2+ Brujah Anarch Barons.** [Aline Gädeke](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/crypt/index.html?card=Aline%20G%C3%A4deke) × 2 (highest cap — ideal Debate target), [Valeriya Zinovieva](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/crypt/index.html?card=Valeriya%20Zinovieva) × 2, [Atiena](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/crypt/index.html?card=Atiena) × 2, [Leumeah](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/crypt/index.html?card=Leumeah) × 2, + singletons.
2. **Expand with [Childe of the Revolution](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Childe%20of%20the%20Revolution) × 4** — Anarch action to put a locked Anarch in play.
3. **Stack [Brujah Debate](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Brujah%20Debate) × 8** on the highest-cap Baron. Each Debate on the vampire: +1 strength, +1 maneuver, but locks the vampire during the Debate. Timing: wait until ≥3 minions ready + 2 Barons before immobilising the Debate host.
4. **Wall.** Organized Resistance × 18 (!), Bait and Switch × 4, Protection Racket × 2, On the Qui Vive × 2.
5. **Payload.** Naked bleeds + [Smiling Jack, The Anarch](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Smiling%20Jack%2C%20The%20Anarch) × 2, [Constant Revolution](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Constant%20Revolution) × 4, [Open War](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Open%20War) × 1 game-changer.
6. **Combat.** Dust Up × 6 + Immortal Grapple × 4 + Taste of Vitae × 4. When Debate stacks, minions become monsters — huge strength + multiple maneuvers take down anyone.
7. **Bloat.** [Villein](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Villein) × 4, [The Parthenon](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=The%20Parthenon) × 2, [Powerbase: Montreal](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Powerbase%3A%20Montreal), [The Rack](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=The%20Rack), [The Anarch Free Press](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=The%20Anarch%20Free%20Press), [Carfax Abbey](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Carfax%20Abbey), [Life Boon](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Life%20Boon), [Wider View](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Wider%20View).
8. **Multi-master-phase enablers.** [The Parthenon](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=The%20Parthenon) × 2 + [Rumors of Gehenna](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Rumors%20of%20Gehenna) × 1 pile the Debate faster.

## Variants

- New archetype — few winners yet.
- Mr. Winthrop / Sport Bike splash for more permanent intercept.
- José Felipe's Consanguineous Boon bloat variant.

## Weaknesses

1. **Debate timing fragile.** Locking Aline too early cripples board; too late and Debate stack doesn't mature.
2. **Flash-oust predators.** Hesha's Emporium / Nephandii finish before Debate stacks.
3. **Stealth bleeds burn the wall.** OR × 18 is a lot but not infinite.
4. **Aline Pentex'd.** Kills the Debate host.
