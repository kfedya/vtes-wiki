# Lutz Politics

Camarilla **stealth-vote + political-bleed** archetype headlined by [Lutz von Hohenzollern](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/crypt/index.html?card=Lutz%20von%20Hohenzollern) (Inner Circle) and [Undele](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/crypt/index.html?card=Undele) (Malkavian Justicar). Achieves a **vote lock** through raw vote totals (Inner Circle + Justicar + Prince) plus [Perfect Paragon](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Perfect%20Paragon) × 6 vote boosters, backed by [Lost in Crowds](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Lost%20in%20Crowds) × 5 and [Forgotten Labyrinth](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Forgotten%20Labyrinth) × 3 stealth for political actions. [Zillah's Valley](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Zillah%27s%20Valley) × 5 accelerates the high-capacity crypt onto the table. Codex Top Tier [src-011].

Canonical decklist: **Kelly Schultz**, NAC 2023, 2023-06-24, TWDA id 10735 [src-011].

## Core idea

1. **Ramp with [Zillah's Valley](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Zillah%27s%20Valley) × 5.** Move 2 blood from pool onto the ready vampire of highest capacity. Net effect: free the next 2 influence-phase transfers for a new vampire.
2. **Seat Inner Circle + Justicar fast.** [Lutz von Hohenzollern](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/crypt/index.html?card=Lutz%20von%20Hohenzollern) × 3 (cap 9, Ventrue Inner Circle — 3 votes) and [Undele](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/crypt/index.html?card=Undele) × 3 (cap 10, Malkavian Justicar — 3 votes) form the engine. [Dmitra Ilyanova](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/crypt/index.html?card=Dmitra%20Ilyanova) × 2 (cap 9, Brujah Justicar) adds another 3 votes and her ability lets you take 1 pool after Minion Tap.
3. **Pool via [Minion Tap](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Minion%20Tap) × 7.** Legacy master card; tap a ready vampire to move blood→pool. Stacks with Voter Captivation and Dmitra's ability for multi-source pool recovery.
4. **Chain referendums.** [Kine Resources Contested](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Kine%20Resources%20Contested) × 6 is the primary pool-drain referendum; [Conservative Agitation](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Conservative%20Agitation) × 3 dumps 2+ pool on any Methuselah; [Banishment](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Banishment) × 3 removes a minion.
5. **Vote-bonus stack.** [Voter Captivation](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Voter%20Captivation) × 8 (+2 votes + 1 pool / 1 blood on success), [Perfect Paragon](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Perfect%20Paragon) × 6 (+2 votes at AUS/PRE), [Bewitching Oration](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Bewitching%20Oration) × 2, [Elder Impersonation](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Elder%20Impersonation) × 3 (+1 vote and stealth), [Awe](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Awe) × 2.
6. **Stealth for political actions.** Political actions are undirected — they can still be blocked. [Lost in Crowds](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Lost%20in%20Crowds) × 5 (+1 stealth OBF), [Forgotten Labyrinth](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Forgotten%20Labyrinth) × 3, [Faceless Night](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Faceless%20Night) × 1.
7. **Emergency votes.** [Political Stranglehold](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Political%20Stranglehold), [Ancilla Empowerment](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Ancilla%20Empowerment), [Reins of Power](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Reins%20of%20Power), [Ancient Influence](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Ancient%20Influence), [Anarchist Uprising](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Anarchist%20Uprising), [Honor the Elders](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Honor%20the%20Elders), [Neonate Breach](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Neonate%20Breach), [Disputed Territory](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Disputed%20Territory) — singleton toolbox of finishers / disruption.
8. **Light defence.** [Force of Personality](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Force%20of%20Personality) × 6 — combat / action mod; exit combat or add stealth on an action. No printed intercept or bounce beyond the crypt's innate AUS/OBF.

## Key disciplines and clans

- **Clan:** Multi-clan — Ventrue (Lutz), Malkavian (Undele), Brujah (Dmitra Ilyanova), Toreador (Rafael de Corazon, Stavros), Lasombra (Orlando Oriundus, Adana de Sforza).
- **Disciplines:** `[AUS]` (Perfect Paragon), `[OBF]` (Lost in Crowds, Elder Impersonation, Faceless Night), `[PRE]` (Voter Captivation, Bewitching Oration, Force of Personality, Awe).
- **Sect:** Camarilla — Inner Circle / Justicar / Prince titles all require Camarilla; vote totals depend on them.

## Support / tech

### Vote lock
Native votes: Lutz × 3 (3 each = 9), Undele × 3 (3 each = 9), Dmitra × 2 (3 each = 6), Prince/other titles. One ready Inner Circle + Justicar contributes 6 votes alone. Perfect Paragon × 6 + Voter Captivation × 8 + Elder Impersonation × 3 + Awe × 2 + Bewitching Oration × 2 provides up to 20+ vote modifiers in deck.

### Pool sustain
Minion Tap × 7 + Voter Captivation +1 pool per cast + Dmitra's ability → pool floor is high.

### Stealth for politics
Political actions are undirected but blockable. OBF stealth stack (Lost in Crowds × 5, Forgotten Labyrinth × 3, Faceless Night × 1) covers the referendum announcements.

### Combat
Force of Personality × 6 is the only combat tech — exits combat + stealth flex. Deck does not engage rush.

## Weaknesses

1. **No combat at all.** Force of Personality × 6 is the entirety of combat defence. Rush predators (Tupdogs, Illegal Brawl) can send key voters to torpor before the lock lands.
2. **Anti-vote tech vulnerability.** [Delaying Tactics](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Delaying%20Tactics), [Parity Shift](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Parity%20Shift), [Scourge of the Enochians](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Scourge%20of%20the%20Enochians) hamstring the plan.
3. **Minion Tap reliance.** No alternative bloat beyond Voter Captivation's +1 pool; if Minion Tap is pushed out, the pool plan stalls.
4. **Legal caveat on Minion Tap.** Minion Tap is in the "removed in 5E" set per [[rulings/cards-removed-in-5e]]; tournament playability varies by format.
