# Salubri Powerbleed

Salubri **powerbleed** archetype built around **[Feast of the Soul's Secrets](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Feast%20of%20the%20Soul%27s%20Secrets) × 6** (action) + [Govern the Unaligned](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Govern%20the%20Unaligned) × 5 paired with an impressive **block-denial** stack. [Seduction](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Seduction) × 6 + [Forced Confessional](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Forced%20Confessional) × 6 + Master-level ([Misdirection](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Misdirection) × 2, [Pentex™ Subversion](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Pentex%E2%84%A2%20Subversion) × 2, [Anarch Troublemaker](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Anarch%20Troublemaker) × 2) paralyze the prey's defences. Salubri's Fortitude enables [Freak Drive](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Freak%20Drive) × 5 for chained actions. Codex New Kid [src-030].

Canonical decklist: **Pasi Karjalainen**, Fee Stake: Jyväskylä 4, 2024-03-02, TWDA id 11271 [src-030].

## Core idea

1. **Seat Salubri crypt.** [Abaddon](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/crypt/index.html?card=Abaddon) × 2, [Seraphina](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/crypt/index.html?card=Seraphina) × 2, [Ilonka](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/crypt/index.html?card=Ilonka) × 2, [Aniel](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/crypt/index.html?card=Aniel) × 2, [Malachi](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/crypt/index.html?card=Malachi) × 2, [Opikun](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/crypt/index.html?card=Opikun), [Yael](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/crypt/index.html?card=Yael).
2. **Ramp with Govern × 5 + Scouting Mission × 4** for stealthy influence.
3. **Block-denial stack.** [Seduction](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Seduction) × 6, [Forced Confessional](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Forced%20Confessional) × 6, [Misdirection](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Misdirection) × 2, Pentex × 2, Anarch Troublemaker × 2, [Unleashing the Bestial Soul](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Unleashing%20the%20Bestial%20Soul) × 2 (makes wake-and-bounce fail).
4. **Powerbleed with Feast of the Soul's Secrets.** Action: big bleed via Dominate. Pair with [Conditioning](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Conditioning) × 2 + [Bonding](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Bonding) × 3 + [Threats](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Threats) × 2.
5. **Action economy.** Freak Drive × 5 (ANI post-action unlock) enables multi-bleed turns.
6. **Defence.** Deflection × 4, Telepathic Misdirection × 2, My Enemy's Enemy × 2, [Murmur of the False Will](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Murmur%20of%20the%20False%20Will) × 2, Eyes of Argus × 3, Delaying Tactics × 2, On the Qui Vive × 2.
7. **Combat.** FOR package: [Rolling with the Punches](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Rolling%20with%20the%20Punches) × 4, [Indomitability](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Indomitability) × 3, [Hidden Strength](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Hidden%20Strength) × 3.
8. **Support.** [The Coven](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=The%20Coven), [Dreams of the Sphinx](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Dreams%20of%20the%20Sphinx), [Meditative Grove](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Meditative%20Grove), [Saulot's Guiding Wisdom](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Saulot%27s%20Guiding%20Wisdom), [Sight Beyond Sight](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Sight%20Beyond%20Sight), [Wash](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Wash) × 2.

## Weaknesses

1. **Archon Investigation vulnerability.**
2. **Breaks against dedicated combat walls** that survive Pentex + Misdirection.
3. **Anti-bleed tech.** Heavy wake-and-bounce stacks (like Malk' 22) need Unleashing the Bestial Soul + Misdirection chain.
4. **Small clan crypt** — Salubri is a niche clan; meta-awareness limited.
