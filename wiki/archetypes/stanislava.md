# Stanislava

Camarilla **stealth-vote + bleed** archetype headlined by **[Stanislava](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/crypt/index.html?card=Stanislava)** (G2 cap 11, Camarilla Gangrel Inner Circle, *ANI/CEL/DOM/FOR/PRO*; printed +2 bleed; retainers employed against her lose abilities; allies cannot block her). Five copies of Stanislava fill most of the crypt. Bleeds and referendums run under a dense Protean stealth/combat-bail stack (Earth Control × 6, Form of Mist × 7, Earth Meld × 5, Instantaneous Transformation × 3, Forced March × 3, Rapid Change × 2). Codex Top Tier [src-017].

Canonical decklist: **Mikey Ferguson**, UK NC 2023, 2023-11-25, TWDA id 10998 [src-017].

## Core idea

1. **Influence [Stanislava](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/crypt/index.html?card=Stanislava) quickly.** Cap 11 vampire; [Zillah's Valley](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Zillah%27s%20Valley) × 8 (2 blood to ready vampire) + [Villein](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Villein) × 6 (3 blood to pool) + [Govern the Unaligned](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Govern%20the%20Unaligned) × 6 land her in 2–3 turns.
2. **Voting engine.** Inner Circle = 3 votes; the secondary crypt ([Hartmut Stover](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/crypt/index.html?card=Hartmut%20Stover) × 2, [Xaviar (ADV)](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/crypt/index.html?card=Xaviar) × 2, [Ingrid Rossler](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/crypt/index.html?card=Ingrid%20Rossler) × 2, [Mark Decker](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/crypt/index.html?card=Mark%20Decker)) adds more votes via titles / bloodpack. [Kine Resources Contested](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Kine%20Resources%20Contested) × 6 is the primary pool-drain referendum.
3. **Stealth with Protean.** [Earth Control](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Earth%20Control) × 6 (+1 stealth PRO), [Forced March](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Forced%20March) × 3 (stealth+unlock action), [Instantaneous Transformation](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Instantaneous%20Transformation) × 3 (stealth + additional action), [Rapid Change](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Rapid%20Change) × 2 (action mod/combat dual).
4. **Bleed on Stanislava's +2 native.** Every Stanislava bleed starts at 3 (1 base + 2 printed). Paired with stealth actions this lands 3–4 pool per turn.
5. **Finisher referenda.** [Anarchist Uprising](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Anarchist%20Uprising), [Ancient Influence](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Ancient%20Influence), [Ancilla Empowerment](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Ancilla%20Empowerment), [Banishment](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Banishment), [Political Stranglehold](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Political%20Stranglehold), [Reins of Power](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Reins%20of%20Power) — singletons.
6. **Defence.** [Deflection](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Deflection) × 6 DOM bounce; [Second Tradition: Domain](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Second%20Tradition%3A%20Domain) × 2 (requires Prince/Justicar — Stanislava's Inner Circle counts for title-based effects). [On the Qui Vive](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=On%20the%20Qui%20Vive) × 2.
7. **Combat bail.** [Form of Mist](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Form%20of%20Mist) × 7 + [Earth Meld](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Earth%20Meld) × 5 (12-card combat module, all Protean bail).
8. **Equipment.** [Enkil Cog](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Enkil%20Cog) × 1 (unique; double actions), [Helicopter](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Helicopter) × 1 (if drawing into an Alastor play), [Seal of Veddartha](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Seal%20of%20Veddartha), [The Signet of King Saul](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=The%20Signet%20of%20King%20Saul).
9. **[Freak Drive](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Freak%20Drive) × 3** — ANI post-action unlock, chain two bleeds per turn.

## Support / tech

### Influence ramp
Zillah's Valley × 8 is extreme — 8/25 master slots. This is the explicit design: ramp the cap-11 crypt as fast as possible, trade first-turn tempo for back-breaking vampire presence.

### Stealth via Protean
The stealth density (6 + 3 + 3 + 2 + 5 combat-bail = ~19 stealth-capable cards) replaces the classic OBF suite. Earth Control + Forced March + Instantaneous Transformation chain into Freak Drive for 2-bleed turns.

### Combat
Pure defensive — Form of Mist × 7 + Earth Meld × 5 = 12 Protean bail cards. Rapid Change × 2 is a combat / action-mod dual that can exit combat *or* add stealth mid-action. No aggressive combat.

### Stanislava-only abilities in play
Her "allies cannot block" and "retainers lose abilities" make her a powerful ally/retainer-meta hedge.

## Variants

- **Classic** — Martin Weinmayer's 2010s builds.
- **Iliana / Caitlin crypt alternatives** — less title but more flexibility.
- **Ashur variant** — adds Ashur Tablets + Parthenon + Liquidation for economy post-2024 balance changes.

## Weaknesses

1. **Slow start.** Cap-11 crypt even with Zillah's Valley × 8 takes 2–3 turns; fast rush/oust decks can finish before Stanislava lands.
2. **Limited bounce.** Deflection × 6 + no Telepathic Misdirection.
3. **Anti-Master disruption.** Wash / Pentex on Zillah's Valley or Villein kneecaps the economy.
4. **Combat-ender decks.** Rötschreck on blocking Stanislava sends her to torpor; Earth Meld doesn't help after combat-ends resolves.
5. **Anti-vote tech.** Delaying Tactics / Parity Shift / Scourge cancellation.
