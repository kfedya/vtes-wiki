# Protean Barons

Multi-clan **Anarch Baron toolbox** built around **[Protean](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Protean) × 6** (Master: the bearer gains `[pro]` at inferior) → any Anarch Baron gets Protean. Then uses [Childe of the Revolution](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Childe%20of%20the%20Revolution) × 8 to bring more Anarch-Baron vampires into play (they inherit Protean via the master), creating an ever-growing swarm that bleeds and defends. Codex Runner-up [src-025].

Canonical decklist: **Vincent Ripoll** (Ankha, the current VEKN Rules Director), French NC 2024, 2024-10-19, TWDA id 11754 [src-025].

## Core idea

1. **Wide Anarch Baron crypt** — 1 copy each: [Sergio Bueno](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/crypt/index.html?card=Sergio%20Bueno), [Ángel Guerrero](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/crypt/index.html?card=%C3%81ngel%20Guerrero), [Massimiliano](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/crypt/index.html?card=Massimiliano), [Valeriya Zinovieva](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/crypt/index.html?card=Valeriya%20Zinovieva), [Vivian VI](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/crypt/index.html?card=Vivian%20VI), [Branimira](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/crypt/index.html?card=Branimira), [Casey Snyder](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/crypt/index.html?card=Casey%20Snyder), [Elisha Tucker](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/crypt/index.html?card=Elisha%20Tucker), [Kuyén](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/crypt/index.html?card=Kuy%C3%A9n), [Kamile Paukstys](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/crypt/index.html?card=Kamile%20Paukstys), [Indira](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/crypt/index.html?card=Indira), [Joseph Fischer](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/crypt/index.html?card=Joseph%20Fischer). Mixed clan: Gangrel, Ministry, Tzimisce, Ravnos, etc.
2. **Drop [Protean](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Protean) × 6 on hosts.** Each Protean Master gives its host `[pro]` access — enabling [Earth Meld](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Earth%20Meld) × 8, [Earth Control](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Earth%20Control) × 4, [Form of the Bat](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Form%20of%20the%20Bat) × 4.
3. **Swarm with [Childe of the Revolution](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Childe%20of%20the%20Revolution) × 8** — Anarch action: put an Anarch vampire from ready into play locked. Inherit Protean master effects via the board.
4. **Wall with [Organized Resistance](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Organized%20Resistance) × 16** (!!) + [Bait and Switch](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Bait%20and%20Switch) × 6 + [Protection Racket](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Protection%20Racket) × 2.
5. **Payload.** Naked bleeds + [Monkey Wrench](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Monkey%20Wrench) × 2 + [Club Illusion](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Club%20Illusion) × 2. Finishers: [Constant Revolution](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Constant%20Revolution) × 1, [Eat the Rich](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Eat%20the%20Rich) × 1, [Revolutionary Council](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Revolutionary%20Council) × 1.
6. **Bloat.** [Villein](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Villein) × 6, [The Anarch Free Press](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=The%20Anarch%20Free%20Press), [Carfax Abbey](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Carfax%20Abbey) × 2, [Papillon](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Papillon), [Garibaldi-Meucci Museum](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Garibaldi-Meucci%20Museum) × 2.
7. **Combat disruption.** [Donnybrook](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Donnybrook) × 6 aggravated, [Earth Meld](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Earth%20Meld) × 8, [Rötschreck](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=R%C3%B6tschreck) × 1.
8. **Utility.** [Aranthebes, The Immortal](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Aranthebes%2C%20The%20Immortal) (unique ally).

## Key disciplines and clans

- **Clans:** Mixed Anarch Barons (Gangrel, Ministry, Tzimisce, Ravnos, Brujah).
- **Disciplines:** `[PRO]` (via Protean master + printed on hosts), `[ANI]`, Anarch-sect payload.
- **Sect:** Anarch — Organized Resistance × 16, Childe of the Revolution × 8, all Baron-gated.

## Variants

- **Simo Tiippana** — original inspiration list.
- **Tommi Hakomaa "Death Star"** — Ashur Tablets + Form of Mist focus on Revolutionary Council.
- **Diego Rodrigues "Rush"** — drop swarm for Bum's Rush + Open War aggressive combat.

Codex notes close similarity to [[archetypes/gangrel-thing|Gangrel Thing]] — fewer intercepts, more voting power.

## Weaknesses

1. **Stealth predators bust.** +2 stealth bleeds through 16 Organized Resistance eventually.
2. **Protean Master contested / Pentexed.**
3. **Fast combo oust.**
4. **Anti-breed event Scourge** — deck can't protect Childe of the Revolution swarm from Scourge.
