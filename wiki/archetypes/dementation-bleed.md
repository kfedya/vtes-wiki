# Dementation Bleed

Malkavian antitribu **stealth-and-bleed** archetype using Dementation (DEM) + Obfuscate (OBF) + Auspex (AUS). Core payload is [Kindred Spirits](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Kindred%20Spirits) × 15 — DEM action, bleed. Bleed modifiers push bleeds to 4–5: [Eyes of Chaos](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Eyes%20of%20Chaos) × 8 (+1 bleed DEM), [Confusion](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Confusion) × 8 (+1 bleed or +1 stealth DEM). Defence is [Telepathic Misdirection](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Telepathic%20Misdirection) × 10 — 2/3 of the crypt has AUS. [Coma](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Coma) × 4 is the combat deterrent (DEM strike → torpor). Codex Runner-up [src-021].

Canonical decklist: **Jonathan da Silva Goudard**, Eternal Vigilance I, 2024-07-07, TWDA id 11567 [src-021].

## Core idea

1. **Seat Malkavian antitribu.** Wide crypt: [Rodolfo](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/crypt/index.html?card=Rodolfo) × 2, [Morel](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/crypt/index.html?card=Morel) × 2, [Midget](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/crypt/index.html?card=Midget) × 2, [Persephone Tar-Anis](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/crypt/index.html?card=Persephone%20Tar-Anis), [The Colonel](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/crypt/index.html?card=The%20Colonel), [Uncle George](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/crypt/index.html?card=Uncle%20George), [Jackie](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/crypt/index.html?card=Jackie), [Marta](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/crypt/index.html?card=Marta), [Cassandra Langely, The Waif](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/crypt/index.html?card=Cassandra%20Langely%2C%20The%20Waif).
2. **Bleed with Kindred Spirits.** 15 copies. DEM action — bleed that costs no blood and the +1 stealth from Confusion.
3. **Stealth stack.** [Confusion](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Confusion) × 8, [Cloak the Gathering](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Cloak%20the%20Gathering) × 5, [Elder Impersonation](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Elder%20Impersonation) × 6, [Faceless Night](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Faceless%20Night) × 4, [Lost in Crowds](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Lost%20in%20Crowds) × 4, [Spying Mission](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Spying%20Mission) × 2, [Swallowed by the Night](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Swallowed%20by%20the%20Night) × 2.
4. **Bleed amp.** Eyes of Chaos × 8 at DEM adds +1 bleed + target-selection trick.
5. **Defence.** Telepathic Misdirection × 10 (AUS bounce), Eyes of Argus × 4 (AUS intercept), My Enemy's Enemy × 2, On the Qui Vive × 4.
6. **Combat deterrent.** Coma × 4 — DEM strike sends opposing vampire to torpor. No rush-heavy combat, just a "don't block" message.
7. **Kindred Spirits pool economy.** The bleed action costs nothing — pure pool damage with stealth.
8. **Extras.** [Revelations](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Revelations) × 2 (emergency anti-bounce), [Vessel](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Vessel) × 2 blood-to-pool, [Institution Hunting Ground](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Institution%20Hunting%20Ground) for blood regen.

## Key disciplines and clans

- **Clan:** Malkavian antitribu (Sabbat offshoot; DEM replaces DOM in signature).
- **Disciplines:** `[DEM]` (Kindred Spirits, Confusion, Eyes of Chaos, Coma), `[OBF]` (Cloak, Lost in Crowds, Faceless Night, Elder Impersonation, Spying Mission), `[AUS]` (Telepathic Misdirection, Eyes of Argus, My Enemy's Enemy, On the Qui Vive).
- **Sect:** Sabbat (antitribu lineage).

## Variants

- **Lower-capacity DEM-only** — [Alessandro Donati](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/crypt/index.html?card=Alessandro%20Donati)'s 2022 Italian NC list for faster gameplay.
- **Louhi variant** — trades speed for [Louhi](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/crypt/index.html?card=Louhi)'s devastating ability.
- **Wash splash** — against heavy intercept locations or bloat decks.

## Weaknesses

1. **Dementation narrow discipline.** No Dominate bounce — relies on Auspex Telepathic Misdirection, which is narrower than Deflection.
2. **No Combat Ends defence.** Coma sends them to torpor but requires contact; combat-enders from prey shut down the strike.
3. **Stealth bust.** Heavy intercept walls burn through Kindred Spirits' printed stealth (none) + stealth-mod stack.
4. **Ward-tech.** Prey's [Ward: Imbued](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Ward%3A%20Imbued) or Sudden Reversal on a key master hurts.
