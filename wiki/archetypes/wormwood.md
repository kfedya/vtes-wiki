# Wormwood

Imbued Hunter **attrition / control** archetype built around the Gehenna event [Wormwood](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Wormwood) as a capacity-decay engine. The deck stacks **14 Gehenna events** (3× Wormwood + 11 other unique events) so that every event played anywhere at the table burns a Wormwood counter. Each burnt counter shrinks the effective capacity of every vampire at the table by 1 — starving bloat/ramp decks of blood capacity while an Imbued wall of convictions + [Second Sight](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Second%20Sight) + [Determine](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Determine) blocks incoming pressure and [Strike with Conviction](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Strike%20with%20Conviction) + [Target Vitals](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Target%20Vitals) converts each block into pool damage to the prey [src-033].

Not a Codex-listed archetype — TWDA fringe that made Top 3 at a 12-player 2R+F event.

Canonical decklist: **Nuno Moure**, Elysium: Almada (Portugal), 2025-05-31, TWDA id 12361 [src-033]. 64-card library (small).

## Core idea

1. **Influence Imbued Hunters.** All-G4 Imbued crypt (12), mixed creeds (Judge/Martyr/Visionary/Defender/Avenger/Redeemer). Imbued have built-in intercept vs. vampires and spend **convictions** (like blood, but a discipline resource) to fire signature cards.
2. **Stockpile convictions.** Each Imbued enters play with 2 convictions; [Leaf Pankowski](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/crypt/index.html?card=Leaf%20%22Potter116%22%20Pankowski) harvests 1/turn during discard; [Angel of Berlin](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Angel%20of%20Berlin) × 5 (Master out-of-turn) gives tempo unlocks and equip-in-response triggers; [The Church of Vindicated Faith](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=The%20Church%20of%20Vindicated%20Faith) pays 1 blood to uncontrolled Imbued per successful action.
3. **Wall the predator.** [Determine](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Determine) × 4 (Vision reaction) redirects bleed to predator's predator OR cancels monster's action card. [Second Sight](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Second%20Sight) × 6 burns conviction for +1 intercept. [React with Conviction](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=React%20with%20Conviction) × 6 cancels any [DOM/PRE/DEM/CHI/SER] strike or directed action at the Imbued.
4. **Cascade Gehenna events.** [Fortschritt Library](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Fortschritt%20Library) × 2 (Master) tutors Gehenna events straight into hand. Every event played anywhere at the table burns 1 Wormwood counter. After ~7 events the entire board has effective cap 3.
5. **Combat-to-bleed finisher.** On a block, chain [Weighted Walking Stick](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Weighted%20Walking%20Stick) × 4 (strength+1 melee weapon, 5 uses) + [Target Vitals](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Target%20Vitals) × 4 (aim: +2 damage AND controller of target loses that pool) + [Strike with Conviction](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Strike%20with%20Conviction) × 6 (aggravated hand/weapon +1). Each successful block → 3–5 pool damage to prey.
6. **Anti-predator tech.** [Aranthebes, The Immortal](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Aranthebes%2C%20The%20Immortal) in play: predator's minions −1 stealth + cap ≤ 4 vampires get −1 bleed vs. you. [Millicent Smith, Puritan Vampire Hunter](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Millicent%20Smith%2C%20Puritan%20Vampire%20Hunter) (Master) migrates to predator in discard; if predator's vampire gets blocked acting, Millicent + vampire both burn without combat.
7. **Event-cancel insurance.** [Not to Be](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Not%20to%20Be) (Master out-of-turn) cancels an event as it is played — hedge against an opponent dropping [Ascension of Caine](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Ascension%20of%20Caine), [Anthelios, The Red Star](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Anthelios%2C%20The%20Red%20Star), or other meta-breakers.

## Key disciplines and creeds

- **Type:** Imbued Hunters (not vampires) — immune to frenzy, bleed-as-monster caveats apply.
- **Creeds:** **Visionary × 2**, **Martyr × 2**, **Judge × 2**, **Defender × 1**, **Avenger × 1**, **Innocent**, **Redeemer**. Multi-creed toolbox.
- **Discipline mix:** `[viz]` (Second Sight, Determine) across Visionaries; `[jud]` (Strike with Conviction, React with Conviction) across Judges; `[inn]` (conviction economy) across Innocent-creed; `[mar]` (Martyrdom) + `[def]` (Defense) tanking.
- **Sect:** N/A — Imbued have no sect.

## Wormwood math

[Wormwood](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Wormwood) enters with 10 counters and requires at least one other Gehenna event already in play. Every subsequent Gehenna event played anywhere on the table burns 1 counter. While Wormwood has X counters, every vampire of printed capacity > X is treated as capacity X (minimum 1).

In a 4-player game with all players sitting on a normal event pace (~1 event per 2 turns), expected Gehenna-event count from opponents alone over a 10-turn game is 4–6, plus 10+ from the Wormwood player itself. That drags effective capacity from 10 → 3–4 by turn 6–8, at which point:

- Cap-11 Inner Circle vampires (Lutz, Mistress Fanchon, Stanislava) cannot carry more than 3 blood → Villein dead, [Minion Tap](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Minion%20Tap) (where legal) ruined.
- Cap-6+ bleeders lose their bleed cushion.
- Big-vampire ramp decks (Govern-chain Malk' 22, Tzimisce Govern Resistance, Baali Unnamed) collapse — their action economy assumes blood pool ≥ 5 on active minions.

The deck does **not** require Wormwood to stick at low counter values; 7–5 counters is already crippling for a Top-Tier bloat archetype.

## Variants

Not a catalogued archetype — single known TWDA finish. Plausible build directions:

- **Deeper Gehenna stack.** Drop combat for 2–3 more unique Gehennas + [Cryptic Mission](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Cryptic%20Mission) / [Unacceptable Menace](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Unacceptable%20Menace) if legal in format, pushing the decay curve faster.
- **Dual-creed focus.** Swap mixed Imbued for all-Judgment crypt + heavier Strike with Conviction combat; shift from control to attrition-bleed hybrid.
- **Weenie vote hybrid.** Add Monkey Wrench / low-cost political actions through Judge/Martyr votes; leans on Maman Boumba's 3-anti-vote swing.

## Weaknesses

1. **Ally-swarm preys.** Wormwood caps **vampires**, not allies. [Nephandii](nephandii.md), [Piper War Ghoul '24](piper-war-ghoul-24.md), and other ally-based decks are untouched by the decay engine.
2. **Weenie-cap decks.** Decks already running cap-1 to cap-3 crypt (Weenie AUS/DOM, Tupdogs, Anarch Baron swarms) don't lose much when capacity shrinks — they were already small.
3. **Rush pressure before stack.** Imbued are medium-pace; the deck needs 5–6 Gehenna plays to meaningfully dent big vampires. A turn-2 rush (Tupdogs, Brujah Line Brawl) can put Imbued in torpor before Angel of Berlin / Leaf harvest enough convictions.
4. **Anti-Imbued tech.** [Ward: Imbued](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Ward%3A%20Imbued) removes the wall directly. [Sense the Savage Way](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Sense%20the%20Savage%20Way) / [Under Siege](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Under%20Siege) disrupt the conviction economy.
5. **Single conviction on Second Sight.** Limited to one burn per action — a vampire that announces a combat-heavy action can outrun the intercept stack once convictions are spent.
6. **Events countered.** A well-timed [Not to Be](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Not%20to%20Be) or [Delay the Will of the Dead](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Delay%20the%20Will%20of%20the%20Dead) from another player can cancel a Wormwood play and preserve big-cap blood pools.
