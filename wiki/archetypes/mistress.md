# Mistress

Camarilla **vote + bleed** archetype named for **[Mistress Fanchon](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/crypt/index.html?card=Mistress%20Fanchon)** (Tremere Inner Circle, cap 11, 3 votes). Her unique ability discounts an **[Alastor](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Alastor) equipped with [Helicopter](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Helicopter)** to 1 pool — the signature combo. Slow, methodical: uses [Villein](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Villein) × 7 + [Zillah's Valley](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Zillah%27s%20Valley) × 3 to seat high-capacity vampires, stays low-profile, then executes a late-game lunge via Parity Shift / Banishment / Political Stranglehold. Codex Top Tier [src-013].

Canonical decklist: **Otso Saariluoma**, EC 2023 Day 1, 2023-10-14, TWDA id 10909 [src-013].

## Core idea

1. **Influence high-cap Inner Circle / Prince crypt.** [Mistress Fanchon](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/crypt/index.html?card=Mistress%20Fanchon) × 3 (Tremere Inner Circle), [Orlando Oriundus](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/crypt/index.html?card=Orlando%20Oriundus) × 2 (Lasombra Prince), [Gabrielle di Righetti](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/crypt/index.html?card=Gabrielle%20di%20Righetti) × 2 provide 8+ votes before modifiers.
2. **Economy backbone.** [Villein](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Villein) × 7 + [Zillah's Valley](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Zillah%27s%20Valley) × 3 + [Information Highway](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Information%20Highway) × 2 + [The Parthenon](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=The%20Parthenon) × 3 + [Ashur Tablets](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Ashur%20Tablets) × 6 → cap-11 Mistress Fanchon lands in 3 turns.
3. **The core combo.** Mistress Fanchon's ability lets her put an [Alastor](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Alastor) (political-action equipment) with a [Helicopter](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Helicopter) on a Red-List vampire for 1 pool. Fetch Helicopter via [Magic of the Smith](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Magic%20of%20the%20Smith) × 2 (THA action).
4. **Vote payload.** [Parity Shift](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Parity%20Shift) × 3 (move 6 pool), [Banishment](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Banishment) × 3 (burn a minion). Singletons: [Political Stranglehold](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Political%20Stranglehold), [Ancient Influence](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Ancient%20Influence), [Ancilla Empowerment](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Ancilla%20Empowerment), [Reins of Power](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Reins%20of%20Power), [Anarchist Uprising](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Anarchist%20Uprising), [Rumors of Gehenna](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Rumors%20of%20Gehenna).
5. **Stealth + redirect.** [Mirror Walk](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Mirror%20Walk) × 6 (THA; stealth or target-redirect), [Forgotten Labyrinth](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Forgotten%20Labyrinth) × 2 (OBF stealth).
6. **Defence via Obedience + Mental Maze + Deflection.** [Obedience](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Obedience) × 4 (OBT reaction: cancel a referendum term or combat-end), [Deflection](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Deflection) × 4 (DOM bounce), [Eyes of Argus](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Eyes%20of%20Argus) × 3 (AUS intercept at cap-10+), [Telepathic Misdirection](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Telepathic%20Misdirection), [My Enemy's Enemy](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=My%20Enemy%27s%20Enemy), [Second Tradition: Domain](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Second%20Tradition%3A%20Domain). [Bowl of Convergence](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Bowl%20of%20Convergence) anti-rush.
7. **Rebuild.** [Ashur Tablets](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Ashur%20Tablets) × 6 — 3rd-copy triggers 13-card tutor.

## Support / tech

### Alastor / Helicopter combo
Magic of the Smith × 2 fetches Helicopter → equip a Red-List opposing vampire via Alastor → 1-pool cost via Fanchon's ability. Helicopter is a 0-cost non-unique equipment that, once placed on a Red-List vampire, lets the acting minion steal blood / actions depending on the ruling. Confirm current ruling on vekn.net/forum.

### Economy backbone
Master count: 32 out of 76 library cards. The deck is built to outlast — survive early pressure and crank Villein on each high-cap seat.

### Rebuild
Ashur Tablets × 6 — three copies in play tuts 13 cards back. With Information Highway × 2 accelerating draws, the rebuild lands around turn 6–7.

### Mirror Walk as swiss-army
Stealth on bleed + redirect on a vote + bounce-like target change for block-induced combat.

### Obedience stack
Obedience × 4 cancels combat-end effects and referendum terms — a flex defensive card that matches the Camarilla Tremere theme. Combined with Deflection × 4 forms the double-bounce stack.

## Variants

- **Malgorzata variant** — swap Mistress Fanchon for [Malgorzata](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/crypt/index.html?card=Malgorzata) for stealth + VIC aggravated damage.
- **Possession variant** — add [The Might of the Camarilla](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=The%20Might%20of%20the%20Camarilla) + [Possession](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Possession) to accelerate Inner Circle seating.
- **A&B enhancement** — more Obedience and Helicopter-fetch tech (Magic of the Smith × 4+).
- **Sidekicks-solo** — if Mistress Fanchon is locked / torpored, Ashur Tablets rebuild ensures the Princes can carry.

## Weaknesses

1. **Slow online.** 32-master pile means the deck needs time to find Parthenon + Villein. Flash-oust decks finish the table before the vote lock fires.
2. **Combat pressure.** Obedience + Bowl of Convergence are the only combat answers; no mass Earth Meld or Mental Maze. Sustained rush (Tupdogs, Illegal Brawl) sends key voters to torpor.
3. **Anti-Master tech.** Wash from Malk' 22 targets Villein directly; multiple Washes stall the economy.
4. **Breed decks specifically.** Scourge × 1 is the only answer; when Scourge is pushed out (event cancellation), breed decks win.
5. **Helicopter ruling nuance.** Using Helicopter on Red-List vampires has specific interactions — requires care in multi-ally / ally-targeting metagames.
