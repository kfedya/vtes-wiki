# Forced Ball

Toreador **political / vote** archetype. The defence isn't stealth but **[Toreador Grand Ball](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Toreador%20Grand%20Ball) × 7** — a Master location-on-vampire whose host cannot be blocked on political actions (each Grand Ball host brings a "guest" vampire into play). Secondary stealth sources [Creepshow Casino](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Creepshow%20Casino), [Camarilla Conclave](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Camarilla%20Conclave), [Perfect Paragon](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Perfect%20Paragon) × 4 also provide stealth mode. [Diana Iadanza](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/crypt/index.html?card=Diana%20Iadanza) × 3 is the key vampire — her ability avoids [Delaying Tactics](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Delaying%20Tactics). [Forced March](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Forced%20March) × 4 chains actions. Codex Runner-up [src-022].

Canonical decklist: **Filippo Tomov**, Italian Tour Trento, 2025-01-11, TWDA id 11913 [src-022].

1. **Seat Toreador with Fortitude.** [Diana Iadanza](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/crypt/index.html?card=Diana%20Iadanza) × 3 (her ability bypasses Delaying Tactics), [Catalina Vega](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/crypt/index.html?card=Catalina%20Vega) × 2, [Flávio Gonçalves](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/crypt/index.html?card=Fl%C3%A1vio%20Gon%C3%A7alves) × 2, [Mkhokheli](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/crypt/index.html?card=Mkhokheli) × 2, + singletons.

## Support / tech

### Grand Ball defence
The Grand Ball host cannot be blocked on political actions — removes the need for stealth modifiers on political actions. The deck runs 7 copies because Grand Balls get destroyed (master-removal) and must be replaced; with high bloat, it just re-buys.

### Diana Iadanza
Her ability avoids Delaying Tactics; at superior Fortitude she unlocks Forced March stealth/action-chain.

### Multi-Grand-Ball stacking
Multiple Grand Balls can stack on one vampire; this doesn't add extra unblockability but protects against losing a single copy to Pentex.

### Sargon Fragment + Grand Ball guests
A Grand Ball's "guest" mechanic lets additional Toreador vampires come into play; Sargon Fragment lets the guest fetch a card from library.

## Variants

- **Closed Session + Camarilla Segregation** — event / political additions.
- **Charismatic Aura** — anti-Immortal-Grapple tech.
- **Without Ashur Tablets** — alternate economy builds.

## Weaknesses

1. **Anti-vote tech.** Delaying Tactics (Diana bypasses), Parity Shift (you run your own), Scourge of the Enochians (against breeds not primary concern).
2. **Grand Ball contested.** If the prey or other opponent attaches a Grand Ball to their vampire (unique location), your Grand Ball may contest — 7 copies mitigate.
3. **Combat pressure.** Majesty × 6 is the only combat answer; sustained rush sends voters to torpor.
4. **Rotation via Pentex.** Pentex Subversion on a Grand Ball host removes the unblockable status.
