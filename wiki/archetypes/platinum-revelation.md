# Platinum Revelation

V5 Ministry **stealth-and-bleed** archetype built around **[The Platinum Protocol](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=The%20Platinum%20Protocol)** (×10) — an Anarch-gated triple-discipline bleed action where `[obf]` / `[pre]` / `[pro]` each add a mode on the *same* play: +1 stealth, +1 bleed, and a corruption counter on a prey's minion on success. The counters feed [Revelation of the Serpent](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Revelation%20of%20the%20Serpent) (bleed amp that burns a counter for a bonus effect) and [Enchanting Gaze](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Enchanting%20Gaze) (burn a counter to permanently prevent a blocker from attempting this action again). [Dabbler](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Dabbler) × 3 converts the triple-discipline chains into blood-or-unlock, out-valuing Villein in a Ministry shell. Codex classifies it as one of 4 ⭐ "Top Dog" current-meta archetypes [src-008].

Canonical decklist: **Jonas Ståhle**, EC 2023 Day 2, 2023-10-15, TWDA id 11032 [src-008].

## Core idea

1. **Seat a Ministry Anarch with all three signatures.** [Vivian VI](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/crypt/index.html?card=Vivian%20VI) × 3 is the flagship. V5 Ministry disciplines are `OBF/PRE/PRO` — exactly what Platinum Protocol demands.
2. **Drop [Dabbler](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Dabbler) × 3** on bleeders. After every successful triple-discipline action, burn 1 blood → unlock, or gain 1 blood. With Platinum Protocol firing `[obf]+[pre]+[pro]`, Dabbler triggers every turn.
3. **Bleed with [The Platinum Protocol](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=The%20Platinum%20Protocol).** At triple-inferior that's +1 stealth, +1 bleed, 1 corruption counter; with discipline upgrades each mode escalates. Corruption counters accumulate on the prey's minions.
4. **Convert counters with [Revelation of the Serpent](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Revelation%20of%20the%20Serpent) × 7.** The base `[pre][pro]` mode is +1 stealth + +1 bleed on the bleed action; at `[PRE][PRO]` the successful bleed also burns 2 of your corruption counters off a prey's minion — translating prior pressure into a bigger current bleed.
5. **Strip blockers with [Enchanting Gaze](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Enchanting%20Gaze) × 4.** At `[PRE][PRO]` a corruption counter on the blocker buys a *permanent* block-fail for this action — no re-block attempt allowed.
6. **Unlock extra actions with [CrimethInc.](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=CrimethInc.) × 5.** Anarch-gated; `[pro]` unlocks the acting vampire, `[qui]` unlocks *another* ready Anarch. Two bleed actions per turn, per vampire.
7. **Defend on Anarch seam.** [Party Out Of Bounds](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Party%20Out%20Of%20Bounds) × 2 is a triple-mode Anarch reaction: `[obf]` -2 to a bleed, `[pre]` +2 votes in polling, `[pro]` +1 intercept. [Bait and Switch](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Bait%20and%20Switch) × 4 bounces. [Form of Mist](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Form%20of%20Mist) × 4 + [Earth Meld](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Earth%20Meld) × 2 bail out of combat and double as stealth on the acting turn.
8. **Cycle and bloat.** [Minion Tap](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Minion%20Tap) × 5 plus Dabbler's blood mode keeps the pool topped; [Anarch Railroad](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Anarch%20Railroad) and [Powerbase: Los Angeles](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Powerbase%3A%20Los%20Angeles) recycle Anarch-flavour cards.

## Key disciplines and clans

- **Clan:** Ministry (V5 rebrand of Followers of Set).
- **Disciplines:** `OBF / PRE / PRO` — every signature Ministry card.
- **Sect:** Anarch — Platinum Protocol, CrimethInc., Party Out Of Bounds, Anarch Railroad, Powerbase: Los Angeles, The Coven, Baron voting all depend on the Anarch sect or Anarch-gated trigger.

## Support / tech

### Stealth stack
- Platinum Protocol `[obf]` mode = +1 stealth on the bleed itself.
- Revelation of the Serpent `[pre][pro]` mode = +1 stealth + +1 bleed.
- Memory Rift × 5, Faceless Night × 1, Lost in Crowds × 1, Spying Mission × 1, True Love's Face × 1 — supplementary stealth modifiers.
- Form of the Bat × 4 (inferior `[aus]` mode) + Form of Mist × 4 (double combat / stealth).
- Swallowed by the Night × 1.

### Corruption-counter engine
- **Sources:** Platinum Protocol `[pro]` mode (1 counter per successful bleed).
- **Sinks:** Revelation of the Serpent `[PRE][PRO]` (burn 2 to remove; bonus effect), Enchanting Gaze `[PRE][PRO]` (burn 1 to cancel a block permanently).

### Block denial
- Enchanting Gaze `[PRE][PRO]` — permanent: blocker cannot re-attempt this action.
- Party Out Of Bounds `[obf]` — -2 bleed fallback on incoming bleeds.

### Economy
- Dabbler × 3 — blood / unlock after every triple-disc action.
- Minion Tap × 5 — legacy Master bloat (still legal in TWDA).
- The Coven — once-per-turn vampire-gain.

### Table disruption
- Mind Numb × 3 — discard 1 card from predator or prey's hand.
- Entrancement × 3 — steal allies for turn or redirect combat.
- Pentex™ Subversion — remove a pesky ally / retainer.

## Variants

- **Apolitical** — remove Barons entirely; rely purely on Party Out Of Bounds for vote defence. Example: **Kim Nilsson**'s decklist [src-008].
- **Master-module tweaks** — add Carfax Abbey and/or Temple Hunting Ground for more blood regeneration; Cave of Apples as a corruption-counter sink.
- **Political-lunge variant** — Eat The Rich + Reckless Agitation + Propaganda of the Deed for post-Protocol voting plays. Less stable but more finishing power.
- **The Unnamed hybrid** — blends Platinum Revelation and [[archetypes/the-unnamed|The unnamed]] stealth-bleed. Example: **Lukáš Simandl**'s Austrian Open submission [src-008].

## Weaknesses

1. **No-Dabbler hands.** Without Dabbler in play, the deck runs on raw blood; the combat/stealth module is expensive to sustain.
2. **Wall preys with cheap intercept.** Wide intercept (Gangrel Thing × 9 Organized Resistance) out-stacks Memory Rift / Platinum Protocol's +1 stealth unless Enchanting Gaze is online to permanently kill a blocker.
3. **Anti-corruption tech.** Prey masters that remove or neutralize corruption counters (Cave of Apples mirror, some rulings-specific removal) starve the Revelation / Enchanting Gaze loop.
4. **Predator stealth-bleeders.** The deck has no bounce beyond Bait and Switch × 4 + Party Out Of Bounds `[obf]`; a predator with heavier stealth packages can outrace the Platinum Protocol tempo.
5. **Vote vulnerability.** No printed vote defence aside from Party Out Of Bounds +2; Reckless Agitation / referendum-heavy pressure threatens the pool line even when the bleed race goes well.
