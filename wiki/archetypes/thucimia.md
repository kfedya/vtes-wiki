# Thucimia

Banu Haqim **stealth-and-bleed** archetype around **[Thucimia](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/crypt/index.html?card=Thucimia)** (G4 cap 10, Banu Haqim, Independent, Blood Cursed, +1 hand size, titled: during a bleed she can give -2 hand size for +1 bleed). The deck stacks **permanent bleed enhancers** on Thucimia via chained actions ([Freak Drive](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Freak%20Drive) × 6, [Forced March](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Forced%20March) × 5, [Dabbler](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Dabbler) × 3, [Enkil Cog](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Enkil%20Cog) × 5) + retainers. [Learjet](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Learjet) × 2 cycles hard. Enkil Cog also bleeds on opponents' turns, sidestepping [Archon Investigation](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Archon%20Investigation). Codex New Kid [src-031] — explicitly notes: "There is no variant to this deck."

Canonical decklist: **Marcin Zakrzewski**, The 29th Disciplines of VTES, 2025-01-17, TWDA id 12029 [src-031].

## Core idea

1. **Stack Thucimia.** [Thucimia](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/crypt/index.html?card=Thucimia) × 6 crypt weight — 2-in-3 opening hands include her.
2. **Permanent bleed enhancers.** [Enkil Cog](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Enkil%20Cog) × 5 (unique action-doubler; activates on opponents' turns too → bypasses Archon), [Dive into Madness](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Dive%20into%20Madness) × 3 (discipline-gain / +bleed permanent), [J.S. Simmons, Esq.](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=J.%20S.%20Simmons%2C%20Esq.), [Tasha Morgan](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Tasha%20Morgan) retainers.
3. **Action chain.** [Freak Drive](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Freak%20Drive) × 6 (ANI post-action unlock) + [Forced March](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Forced%20March) × 5 (FOR unlock) + [Dabbler](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Dabbler) × 3 (Master trifle: blood/unlock after triple-discipline action).
4. **Library cycling.** [Learjet](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Learjet) × 2 (electronic equipment, cycle entire library fast).
5. **Bleed payload.** [Kindred Spirits](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Kindred%20Spirits) × 7 (DEM bleed), [Loss](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Loss) × 5 (NEC action), [Khabar: Glory](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Khabar%3A%20Glory) × 3 (Banu Haqim title gain).
6. **Bleed amp.** [Confusion](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Confusion) × 6, [Eyes of Chaos](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Eyes%20of%20Chaos) × 5, [Deed the Heart's Desire](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Deed%20the%20Heart%27s%20Desire) × 6 (cancel bounce), [Touch of Clarity](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Touch%20of%20Clarity) × 3 (anti-bounce mod/reaction).
7. **Stealth.** [Lost in Crowds](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Lost%20in%20Crowds) × 6, [Confusion](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Confusion) × 6 (doubles as bleed amp), [Elder Impersonation](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Elder%20Impersonation) × 3, [Faceless Night](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Faceless%20Night) × 3, [Swallowed by the Night](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Swallowed%20by%20the%20Night) × 3, [Spying Mission](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Spying%20Mission) × 1, [Deny](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Deny) × 1.
8. **Defence.** [Secure Haven](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Secure%20Haven) × 4 (primary anti-rush — protects Thucimia), [Direct Intervention](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Direct%20Intervention) × 2 (safe Enkil Cog bleeds + prevent contest), [Wash](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Wash) × 2 (anti-intercept-location + anti-bloat), [Confusion of the Eye](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Confusion%20of%20the%20Eye) × 2. Events: [The Bitter and Sweet Story](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=The%20Bitter%20and%20Sweet%20Story), [Scourge of the Enochians](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Scourge%20of%20the%20Enochians).

## Support / tech

### Enkil Cog + Archon sidestep
Enkil Cog lets Thucimia bleed on opponents' turns. Archon Investigation triggers only on your turn, so off-turn Enkil Cog bleeds stay below Archon's notice.

### Chained actions
Freak Drive × 6 (ANI) + Forced March × 5 (FOR) + Dabbler × 3 (Master) + Enkil Cog × 5 stacked = potentially 4–5 actions per turn for Thucimia.

### Anti-bounce
Deed the Heart's Desire × 6, Touch of Clarity × 3, Direct Intervention × 2, Confusion of the Eye × 2 — dense counter-bounce.

## Variants

Per Codex: **"There is no variant to this deck."** The deck's single-engine build is considered definitive [src-031].

## Weaknesses

1. **Thucimia contested.** Unique high-cap; Pentex risk.
2. **Blood Cursed trait** — cannot be diablerised, but still costs 1 pool/turn.
3. **Rush on the hand-size hit.** Her -2 hand size cost leaves her thin; combined with rush pressure, the deck fumbles.
4. **Heavy intercept walls.** Stealth stack can be exhausted.
