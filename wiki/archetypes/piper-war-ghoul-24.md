# Piper War Ghoul '24

Tzimisce **rush / ally-combat** archetype built around **[War Ghoul](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=War%20Ghoul) × 6** as the primary threat. [Piper](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Piper) × 7 is the Master that turns low-capacity Tzimisce vampires into War Ghoul recruiters; [Jake Washington](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Jake%20Washington) × 7 (Master) adds cheap ally fodder. [Dragonbound](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Dragonbound) event + unblocked bleeds on toyed-down preys finish the oust. Codex New Kid [src-029].

Canonical decklist: **Alessandro Donati**, Italian Tour Bologna, 2024-09-15, TWDA id 11590 [src-029].

## Core idea

1. **Low-cap Tzimisce + Anarch Convert crypt.** [Susie Kano](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/crypt/index.html?card=Susie%20Kano) × 3, [Anarch Convert](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/crypt/index.html?card=Anarch%20Convert) × 3, [Marialena](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/crypt/index.html?card=Marialena) × 2, [Branimira](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/crypt/index.html?card=Branimira), [Clara Hjortshøj](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/crypt/index.html?card=Clara%20Hjortsh%C3%B8j), [Whisper](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/crypt/index.html?card=Whisper), [Prentis Derby](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/crypt/index.html?card=Prentis%20Derby).
2. **Stack [Piper](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Piper) × 7** (Master) — grants the Tzimisce access to War Ghoul recruit.
3. **[Jake Washington](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Jake%20Washington) × 7** (Master ally) — cheap fodder for burning into War Ghoul construction.
4. **[War Ghoul](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=War%20Ghoul) × 6 — primary threat.** Large allies; rush-combat oriented.
5. **Big allies.** [Ossian](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Ossian), [Vozhd of Sofia](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Vozhd%20of%20Sofia), [Asanbonsam Ghoul](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Asanbonsam%20Ghoul), [Carlton Van Wyk](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Carlton%20Van%20Wyk) (anti-diablerie), [Vagabond Mystic](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Vagabond%20Mystic).
6. **Retainers.** [J. S. Simmons, Esq.](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=J.%20S.%20Simmons%2C%20Esq.), [Tasha Morgan](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Tasha%20Morgan), [Jackie Therman](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Jackie%20Therman), [Mr. Winthrop](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Mr.%20Winthrop) — intercept.
7. **Combat.** [Fake Out](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Fake%20Out) × 2 + [Trap](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Trap) × 3 + [Donnybrook](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Donnybrook) × 3.
8. **Defence.** [Deflection](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Deflection) × 6, [Deep Ecology](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Deep%20Ecology) × 3, [On the Qui Vive](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=On%20the%20Qui%20Vive) × 3, [One With the Land](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=One%20With%20the%20Land) × 3, [Delaying Tactics](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Delaying%20Tactics) × 1. Scales to 4 intercept.
9. **Events.** [Dragonbound](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Dragonbound) × 1 (pool damage), [The Unmasking](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=The%20Unmasking) × 2 (ally boost). [Direct Intervention](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Direct%20Intervention) emergency anti-Entrancement.
10. **Failsafe + Fame + Powerbase: Montreal** misc.

## Weaknesses

1. **Political decks**. Deck struggles vs vote — prioritise voter removal.
2. **Ally-hate tech.** Pentex, Anarch Troublemaker, Ward: Imbued.
3. **Table attention.** War Ghouls draw aggro — keep predator alive for bounces.
4. **60-card library is small** — draws fast but runs out against stall decks.
