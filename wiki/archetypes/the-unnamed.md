# The unnamed

Baali **stealth-and-bleed + bloat** archetype built around **[The unnamed](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/crypt/index.html?card=The%20unnamed)** (G6, cap 10, Baali Independent, Infernal) — every successful bleed gains you 2 pool. With [Enkil Cog](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Enkil%20Cog) (double the action) and [Homunculus](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Homunculus) (retainer that adds +1 bleed) attached, the unnamed bleeds twice per turn at +2 bleed *and* returns 4 pool to your pool. Codex Top Tier [src-018].

Canonical decklist: **Darby Keeney**, Origins Friday 2, 2024-06-21, TWDA id 11442 [src-018].

## Core idea

1. **Seat [The unnamed](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/crypt/index.html?card=The%20unnamed).** Cap 10 Baali, Infernal trait (1 pool lost per unlock phase). 5 copies in crypt; multiple bleeders maintain the +2-pool-per-bleed engine even under rush pressure.
2. **Attach [Enkil Cog](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Enkil%20Cog) × 3 + [Homunculus](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Homunculus) × 3.** Enkil Cog doubles the action; Homunculus gives +1 bleed as a retainer. Each Unnamed bleed becomes 2× (3 bleed) and +4 pool on the way.
3. **Flood with [Flurry of Action](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Flurry%20of%20Action) × 6.** Action card: unlock this vampire after resolution; chain multiple actions.
4. **[Tend the Flock](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Tend%20the%20Flock) × 6.** Master: choose between 1 pool + 1 vampire influence or 4 pool to self. Flexible bloat.
5. **Stealth with Obfuscate + Infernal-Themed modifiers.** [Instantaneous Transformation](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Instantaneous%20Transformation) × 6 (+1 stealth + additional action PRO), [Swallowed by the Night](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Swallowed%20by%20the%20Night) × 5, [Revelation of the Serpent](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Revelation%20of%20the%20Serpent) × 3, [I am Legion](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=I%20am%20Legion) × 3 (DAI/OBF bounce-cancel and +1 pool), [Faceless Night](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Faceless%20Night) × 2, [Lost in Crowds](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Lost%20in%20Crowds) × 2, [Elder Impersonation](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Elder%20Impersonation) × 2.
6. **Combat with Infernal allies.** [Infernal Servitor](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Infernal%20Servitor) × 3 (Infernal ally; blocker/attacker), [Veneficti](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Veneficti) × 1, [Mylan Horseed](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Mylan%20Horseed) × 1, [D'habi Revenant](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=D%27habi%20Revenant) retainer.
7. **Finisher: [Unleash Hell's Fury](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Unleash%20Hell%27s%20Fury) × 3.** DAI/PRE action: +1 bleed + choose a mode (damage prey minion 2R, cancel opposing mod, etc.).
8. **Bounce-piercing.** [The Horde](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/crypt/index.html?card=The%20Horde) × 3 in crypt + [Contagion](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Contagion) × 2 help get around [Party Out Of Bounds](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Party%20Out%20Of%20Bounds) / [Visions of Zapathasura](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Visions%20of%20Zapathasura) reduce-bleed reactions.
9. **Defence.** [Eluding the Arms of Morpheus](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Eluding%20the%20Arms%20of%20Morpheus) × 4 (wake / action-unlock reaction), [Majesty](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Majesty) × 3 combat-end, [Form of the Bat](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Form%20of%20the%20Bat) × 2, [Sense the Sin](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Sense%20the%20Sin) × 2, [Psychomachia](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Psychomachia) × 2, [Approximation of Loyalty](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Approximation%20of%20Loyalty) × 2.

## Support / tech

### Pool engine
The unnamed's +2-pool-per-bleed + Tend the Flock × 6 + Flurry of Action × 6 + Homunculus × 3 (each adds +1 bleed → +1 pool gained on return) creates a pool-positive bleed loop that absorbs Infernal trait's 1 pool/turn tax.

### Double bleed via Enkil Cog
Enkil Cog (unique equipment) doubles the action. Playing Enkil Cog + Instantaneous Transformation chains two bleeds with stealth.

### The Horde bypass
The Horde is non-unique and can be repeatedly put into play (cap 3); locking Horde during the bleed gives a target infernal +1 bleed. This "extra bleed" bypasses bleed-reduction reactions like Party Out Of Bounds (-2 bleed) because the +1 comes from Horde, not from an action-mod.

### Infernal allies
Infernal Servitor × 3 attacks or defends. D'habi Revenant retainer prevents first-strike damage or enables combat extensions.

## Variants

- **Anarch Unnamed** — add Platinum Protocol + Ministry sidekicks. Hybrid with [[archetypes/platinum-revelation|Platinum Revelation]] — see **Lukáš Simandl**'s Austrian Open build referenced on that page.
- **Hell Rising** — add [The Rising](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=The%20Rising) to limit opponent bloat.
- **Maleficia** — curse/evil-eye mechanics on top of the bleed plan.
- **Coven-focused** — extra blood-regeneration via [The Coven](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=The%20Coven).

## Weaknesses

1. **Infernal tax.** 1 pool per unlock phase per Infernal vampire = -5 to -7 per turn when crypt is wide. The pool engine must stay online.
2. **Ally-hate tech.** Pentex on Infernal Servitor or Homunculus stalls.
3. **Wash on Tend the Flock.** The master's a non-unique Master that can be Washed out.
4. **Rush combo decks.** Hesha's Emporium ousts before the Infernal vampire count matures.
5. **Light combat.** Majesty × 3 is the only combat card for vampires — Unnamed relies on Infernal allies to take hits.
