# Gangrel Thing

Gangrel Anarch **wall / grind-bleed** archetype. Influences 4–6 cheap Anarch Gangrels via repeated **[Thing](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Thing)** (Anarch action: put a vampire of capacity up to 3 from ready region into play locked). Wins by **naked bleeds** across a wide table, boosted by **[Smiling Jack, The Anarch](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Smiling%20Jack%2C%20The%20Anarch)** and **[Constant Revolution](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Constant%20Revolution)**, while a stack of intercept reactions (**Organized Resistance × 9, Deep Ecology × 5, Eyes of the Wild × 2**) plus **Bait and Switch × 5** deflects incoming aggression [src-005]. Classified by Codex of the Damned as one of 4 ⭐ "Top Dog" archetypes in the current meta [src-005].

Listed as top-tier based on consistent TWDA finishes in 2023–2024: **Marius Iscru** (2024 VtES European Last Chance Qualifier, TWDA id 11667 — the deck reproduced below), **Édouard Menneson** (2024 Iberan GP, id 11174, Brutal variant), **Otso Saariluoma** (Ropecon 2023, id 10767, Brutal variant), **Jakub Rypar** (2024 Slovak NC, id 11740, Council Propaganda variant), **José Roberio Barbosa da Silva** (2024 SAC, id 11393, Renegade variant).

## Core idea

1. **Seat a Baron fast.** [Kuyén](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/crypt/index.html?card=Kuy%C3%A9n) (G6, cap 6, Baron, `ANI/PRO`) is the flagship 3-of; she influences on turn 2–3 and immediately contributes 2 votes to the table while unlocking intercept reactions that cost Anarch. If the opening hand is slow, a cheaper vampire goes in first to cushion [Thing](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Thing) plays.
2. **Spam [Thing](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Thing)** (5 copies). Each successful Thing action brings a cap-≤3 vampire into play locked, growing the ready pool to 4–6 minions.
3. **Harden the wall.** Locations fetch permanent effects: [Powerbase: Montreal](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Powerbase%3A%20Montreal) and [The Anarch Free Press](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=The%20Anarch%20Free%20Press) cycle and add intercept on Anarch plays; [Garibaldi-Meucci Museum](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Garibaldi-Meucci%20Museum) tutors the right reaction; [Club Illusion](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Club%20Illusion) × 2 adds stealth.
4. **Grind bleed.** Small naked bleeds across 4–6 ready Anarchs produce steady pool damage. [Smiling Jack](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Smiling%20Jack%2C%20The%20Anarch) converts Anarchs-in-play into a recurring 1-pool-per-unlock tax on the prey. [Constant Revolution](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Constant%20Revolution) doubles the bleed count of naked pressure.
5. **Lunge on command.** [Reckless Agitation](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Reckless%20Agitation) (political, direct pool damage) and [Monkey Wrench](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Monkey%20Wrench) (+bleed action mod) convert a slow grind into a finishing lunge.
6. **Combat on their terms.** [Earth Meld](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Earth%20Meld) × 11 and [Form of Mist](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Form%20of%20Mist) × 4 bail out of unfavourable combats; [Form of the Bat](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Form%20of%20the%20Bat) × 5 doubles as stealth or intercept. [Sniper Rifle](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Sniper%20Rifle) × 2 turns blocked lunges into grind beads.

## Key disciplines and clans

- **Clan:** Gangrel — 9 of 12 crypt slots are Gangrel Anarch Barons.
- **Disciplines:** `ANI` (Deep Ecology, Raven Spy, Organized Resistance's Anarch requirement is satisfied by Baron vampires), `PRO` (Form of Mist, Earth Meld, Deep Ecology), `FOR` (Deep Ecology), `for` (Eyes of the Wild). Minor splash: `[CEL]` from Nathan Turner, `[AUS]` for Form of the Bat double-mode.
- **Sect:** Anarch — required for Thing, Smiling Jack, Constant Revolution, The Anarch Free Press, Powerbase: Montreal bonus, Baron title voting, and the broader Revolutionary Council variant package. See [[entities/sects/anarch|anarch]].

## Support / tech

### Intercept stack
1. **Organized Resistance × 9** — Anarch reaction, +1 intercept at superior; the primary wall card. Baron vampires satisfy its Anarch-only printed requirement trivially.
2. **Deep Ecology × 5** — `ani/for/pro`; +1 intercept and cycles.
3. **Eyes of the Wild × 2** — `for`; +1 intercept, recycles into hand on successful block.
4. **Form of the Bat × 5** — double mode; at inferior `[aus]` it's +1 stealth on actions, at superior `[pro]` it's +2 intercept reaction.
5. **Raven Spy × 4** — retainer +1 intercept, soaks one damage.
6. **The Anarch Free Press** + **Garibaldi-Meucci Museum** — master-phase tutors for the right intercept/bounce.

### Bounce
- **Bait and Switch × 5** — `aus/cel/for/pro`; the universal Gangrel-accessible bounce.

### Pool recovery / cycle
- **Villein × 3** — blood-to-pool on a ready vampire; fine on fat Baron hosts.
- **Vessel × 2** — per-unlock pool gain on the vessel-bearer.
- **Life Boon** — single-copy event-reuse prey-edge mitigation.
- **Powerbase: Montreal** — recycle discarded Anarch cards back into the deck.

### Lunge
- **Constant Revolution** — action; every Anarch in play bleeds for +1 this turn on the next bleed. With 4–6 Anarchs in play it triples–quadruples a naked bleed.
- **Reckless Agitation** — referendum; pool damage regardless of opponent's bounce.
- **Monkey Wrench** — `—` action mod; +2 bleed on a naked bleed action.

### Location denial
- **Rewilding** — action; burn target location, dealing 1 pool damage if location belonged to prey. Hits permanent stealth (Opium Den, Creepshow Casino) and adverse hunting grounds.

## Weaknesses

1. **Stealth-heavy powerbleed preys** — the wall is intercept-based, not stealth-cancellation; decks packing ≥8 printed stealth (Malk' 22, Ravnos Bonds, The unnamed) can burn past Organized Resistance before the defender cycles a second copy.
2. **Flash-oust decks** — Hesha's Emporium and similar combo decks can oust before the Gangrel table matures (turns 3–4).
3. **Low pool buffer** — 30-pool start + small incremental recovery (Villein, Vessel). A single successful 5-bleed by a predator is hard to recover from in the mid-game, since Bait and Switch only bounces — it doesn't gain pool.
4. **Limited stealth** — naked bleeds face blocks; lunging relies on numeric pressure rather than punching through a single intercept card. Against wall grandprey / wall prey the deck can stall.
