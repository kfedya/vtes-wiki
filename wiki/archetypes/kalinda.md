# Kalinda

Banu Haqim **stealth-and-bleed** archetype centred on **[Kalinda (G6)](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/crypt/index.html?card=Kalinda)** — her ability lets her bleed then unlock, allowing a defensive [Protected District](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Protected%20District) block on the predator's turn. Uses [Haqim's Law: Retribution](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Haqim%27s%20Law%3A%20Retribution) × 6 for bleed amp (discard combat card for +1 bleed) — same mechanic as [[archetypes/haqim-royalty|Haqim Royalty]], differentiated by the stealth-over-vote plan. Codex New Kid [src-027].

Canonical decklist: **Bram Van Stappen**, New Years Blood 2025, 2025-01-12, TWDA id 11997 [src-027].

## Core idea

1. **Seat [Kalinda (G6)](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/crypt/index.html?card=Kalinda) × 4.** Her ability: bleed → unlock, lets her swing offence-then-defence in a turn cycle. Support crypt: [Flavia, Avenging Angel](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/crypt/index.html?card=Flavia%2C%20Avenging%20Angel), [Hafsa, The Watcher](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/crypt/index.html?card=Hafsa%2C%20The%20Watcher), [Kassandra Tassaki](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/crypt/index.html?card=Kassandra%20Tassaki), [Khadija Al-Kindi](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/crypt/index.html?card=Khadija%20Al-Kindi), [Warmaksan](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/crypt/index.html?card=Warmaksan), [Wise Frog](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/crypt/index.html?card=Wise%20Frog), [Adisa](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/crypt/index.html?card=Adisa), [Alu](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/crypt/index.html?card=Alu).
2. **Drop [Haqim's Law: Retribution](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Haqim%27s%20Law%3A%20Retribution) × 6** early. Each Assamite bleed can discard a combat card for +1 bleed.
3. **Defensive cornerstone: [Protected District](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Protected%20District) × 7** — prevents bleeds on you while this vampire is in your uncontrolled region. Kalinda's bleed-then-unlock ability keeps her ready for the reaction.
4. **Stealth stack.** [Swallowed by the Night](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Swallowed%20by%20the%20Night) × 9 (dual stealth/combat, also discard fuel for Retribution), [Resist Earth's Grasp](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Resist%20Earth%27s%20Grasp) × 2 (same dual role), [Lost in Crowds](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Lost%20in%20Crowds) × 7, [Faceless Night](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Faceless%20Night) × 3, [Cloak the Gathering](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Cloak%20the%20Gathering) × 2, [Elder Impersonation](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Elder%20Impersonation) × 2, [Spying Mission](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Spying%20Mission) × 2, [Domain of Evernight](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Domain%20of%20Evernight) × 1.
5. **Political lunge.** [Camarilla's Iron Fist](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Camarilla%27s%20Iron%20Fist) × 3 (pool drain), [Banu Haqim Justicar](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Banu%20Haqim%20Justicar) × 1 (title-gain + pool), [Rumors of Gehenna](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Rumors%20of%20Gehenna) × 1.
6. **Combat.** [Hunger of Marduk](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Hunger%20of%20Marduk) × 5 (THA ranged steal blood), [Pursuit](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Pursuit) × 4, [Infernal Pursuit](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Infernal%20Pursuit) × 2, [Side Strike](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Side%20Strike) × 2, [Behind You!](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Behind%20You%21) × 1, [No Trace](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=No%20Trace) × 1, [Preternatural Evasion](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Preternatural%20Evasion) × 1, [Torrent](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Torrent) × 1. Mostly defensive maneuvers/dodges.
7. **Economy.** [Villein](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Villein) × 4, [Ashur Tablets](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Ashur%20Tablets) × 6, [Wider View](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Wider%20View) × 2, [The Coven](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=The%20Coven), [Alamut](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Alamut).
8. **Metagame**: looks like Haqim Royalty → opponents don't rush Kalinda for fear of combat retaliation.

## Weaknesses

1. **Protected District unique.** One copy → contest risk. 7 redundancy.
2. **Anti-stealth walls.** +stealth eventually loses to +3 intercept.
3. **Pool slow.** No massive bloat beyond Villein × 4 / Ashur trigger.
