# Malkarishat

**Malkavian Justicar + Baali** vote archetype. Name is portmanteau of **Juliet Parr (Malkavian Justicar)** + **Arishat (Baali Baron)**. Juliet Parr's ability gives +2 hand size after her referendums pass; Arishat's ability forces abstention on a vampire during polling. Pair this with dense stealth (Forgotten Labyrinth × 7, Lost in Crowds × 7) and the classic Camarilla referendum chain. Codex New Kid [src-028].

Canonical decklist: **Danilo Torrisi**, EC 2024 Day 2, 2024-09-29, TWDA id 11669 [src-028].

## Core idea

1. **Seat [Juliet Parr](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/crypt/index.html?card=Juliet%20Parr) × 4 + [Arishat](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/crypt/index.html?card=Arishat) × 4.** Juliet is G7 Camarilla Malkavian Justicar (3 votes + post-pass +2 hand size + 1 maneuver/combat). Arishat is G6 Baali Baron Infernal (1 vote + polling abstention).
2. **Support crypt:** [Alexander Silverson](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/crypt/index.html?card=Alexander%20Silverson) × 2, [Gilbert Duane (G6)](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/crypt/index.html?card=Gilbert%20Duane), [Andi Liu](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/crypt/index.html?card=Andi%20Liu).

4. **Referendum chain.** [Kine Resources Contested](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Kine%20Resources%20Contested) × 6, [Parity Shift](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Parity%20Shift) × 6, [My Kin Against the World](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=My%20Kin%20Against%20the%20World) × 2, [Anarchist Uprising](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Anarchist%20Uprising), [Ancilla Empowerment](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Ancilla%20Empowerment).
5. **Vote-lock ability.** [Condemnation: Mute](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Condemnation%3A%20Mute) × 4 (DAI — strip a minion's votes) + [Unleash Hell's Fury](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Unleash%20Hell%27s%20Fury) × 2 (DAI action).

## Weaknesses

1. **No wake effects** — defence requires proactive minion unlocking.
2. **Infernal tax on Arishat.** -1 pool per Arishat per unlock phase.
3. **Contested Juliet Parr** — unique and high-cap; Pentex or diablerie risk.
4. **Anti-vote tech.** Delaying Tactics, Parity Shift mirror.
