# Banned Cards in VEKN Sanctioned Play

The VEKN Tournament Rules §4.1 list the cards that may **not** appear in any deck at a sanctioned tournament — Constructed *or* Limited [src-032]. Inclusion of any banned card is grounds for the head judge to apply the VEKN Penalty Guidelines.

## Ante-card ban [src-032 §4.1]

Any card that uses the **ante** mechanic is banned. Examples called out by name in the rules:

- *[Cunctator Motion](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Cunctator%20Motion)* (V:TES)
- *[High Stakes](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=High%20Stakes)* (V:TES)
- *[Playing for Keeps](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Playing%20for%20Keeps)* (Dark Sovereigns)

The ban is **categorical** — *any* card with "ante" in its rules text is banned, even cards not specifically listed above (see Appendix B *Ante Card* definition [src-032]).

## Notes

- **Nights of Reckoning rules cards are legal** in both Constructed and Limited [src-032 §4.1]. Only *Edge Explosion* from that set is banned.
- The VEKN reserves the right to amend this list without notice [src-032 §1.7]. Cross-check the live rules document at <https://www.vekn.net/tournament-rules/4-tournament-game-rules> before using this page as deck-construction reference for a real event.
