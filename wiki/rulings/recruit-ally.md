# Recruit Ally

## Summary
Action to bring an **ally** into play. Allies are non-vampire minions that act and block independently of the recruiter [src-001]. See [[ally]].

## Action Profile [src-001]
- **Who can recruit:** any ready minion.
- **Cost:** as listed on the ally card.
- **Default target:** none. **Undirected** action.
- **Default stealth:** +1.
- **Effect:** on success, the ally is placed in your ready region. Blood counters equal to the ally's listed life are placed on it from the blood bank. An ally **cannot act on the turn it is recruited** ("summoning sickness"). When an ally loses its last life counter, it is burned.

## Discipline-Requirement Quirk (Advanced)
If recruiting the ally requires a Discipline and the ally is put into play by some **other means** (e.g., a card effect that puts an ally into play directly), use the **basic** version. Allies brought into play by other means **can act on the same turn** — the recruit-action-only sickness rule does not apply [src-001 p. 22].

## Common Cards Involved
- [Underbridge Stray](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Underbridge%20Stray) — example ally used throughout rulebook.
- [Carlton Van Wyk](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Carlton%20Van%20Wyk) — classic hunter ally.

## Common Mistakes
- Having a freshly-recruited ally act the same turn (they can't, unless put into play by non-recruit means).
- Treating allies as vampires for "as a vampire" card requirements — they are not, except where [[ally]]'s "as a vampire" rule applies.
- Forgetting that an ally's life counters come from the **blood bank**, not the recruiter's blood.
