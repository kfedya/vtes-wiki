# Employ Retainer

## Summary
Action to put a **retainer** card on the acting minion. Retainers have life counters from the blood bank; unlike equipment, they cannot be transferred later [src-001]. See [[retainer]].

## Action Profile [src-001]
- **Who can employ:** any ready minion.
- **Cost:** as listed on the retainer card.
- **Default target:** none. **Undirected** action.
- **Default stealth:** +1.
- **Effect:** on success, the retainer is placed on the acting minion with blood from the bank equal to its listed life. No limit on how many retainers a minion can carry. If a retainer runs out of life counters, it is **burned**.

## Discipline-Requirement Quirk (Advanced)
If employing the retainer requires a Discipline and the retainer is put into play by **some other means**, use the **basic** version of the retainer [src-001].

## Retainers in Combat
A retainer is **not normally harmed** in combat. However, at **long range**, an attacker can announce their strike as targeting an opposing minion's retainer instead of the minion itself — announced when the strike is announced [src-001 p. 33].

A retainer that inflicts damage (e.g., Murder of Crows, Carrion Crows) deals **environmental** damage — no minion-source, so cannot be dodged [src-001 pp. 31, 50].

## Common Cards Involved
- [Murder of Crows](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Murder%20of%20Crows) — retainer that inflicts environmental damage (works like Carrion Crows) [src-001 p. 50].
- [Underbridge Stray](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Underbridge%20Stray) — example retainer from rulebook illustrations.

## Common Mistakes
- Treating retainers as transferable like equipment — they are not.
- Dodging environmental damage from a retainer — you can't; dodge only protects against the opponent's strike.
- Forgetting the retainer is burned when its employer is burned (see [[retainer]]).
