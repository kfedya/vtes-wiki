# Political Action

## Summary
A vampire-only action to **call a referendum**. Always **undirected**. If the action succeeds, the referendum is called — but the referendum can still **pass or fail** independently of the action's success [src-001].

## Action Profile [src-001]
- **Who can take a political action:** any ready **vampire**. A vampire **cannot perform more than one political action per turn** (even if they unlock).
- **Cost:** as listed on the political action card.
- **Default target:** none. **Undirected** (always — even if the referendum terms mention another Methuselah).
- **Default stealth:** +1.
- **Effect:** on success, cost is paid and the referendum is called; see [[referendum]].

## Crucial FAQ — Action vs Referendum [src-001 p. 47]
> If the referendum of a political action fails, is the action considered successful?
> The political action is successful as long as it is not blocked, even if the referendum fails.

This matters for cards that trigger "if this action is successful" or "after a successful action" (e.g., Freak Drive after a political action). The referendum result is separate.

## Announcing the Terms (Delayed)
> The specific terms of the referendum (the choices to be made, if any, by the Methuselah who calls the referendum) are not announced until the action itself is successful. [src-001 p. 27]

This is an exception to the general rule that all details are set at action announcement. Practical effect: opponents decide whether to block **without knowing the exact terms**.

## Common Cards Involved
- Any political action card (e.g., [Kine Resources Contested](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Kine%20Resources%20Contested), [Ancilla Empowerment](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Ancilla%20Empowerment), [Consanguineous Boon](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Consanguineous%20Boon)).
- [Kine Resources Contested](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Kine%20Resources%20Contested) — can't dump all 4 points onto one Methuselah; can point at yourself if needed [src-001 p. 49].
- [Ancilla Empowerment](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Ancilla%20Empowerment) — **every** Methuselah loses pool, including you [src-001 p. 47].
- [Consanguineous Boon](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Consanguineous%20Boon) — must name an existing clan even if no vampires of that clan are in play [src-001 p. 47].
- [Freak Drive](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Freak%20Drive) — unlocks after a successful action, including a political action whose referendum failed [src-001 p. 49].

## Common Mistakes
- Treating the action as directed because the terms will name a specific Methuselah — political actions are **always undirected**; prey and predator decide blocks.
- Equating referendum failure with action failure — the action can be fully successful (cost paid, resolved) and the referendum still fails.
- Letting a vampire call two political actions in one turn via unlock effects — not allowed.
