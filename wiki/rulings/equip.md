# Equip

## Summary
An action to put an **equipment** card onto the acting minion, either from hand (paying the equipment's cost) or from another of the acting Methuselah's minions (no cost) [src-001]. See [[equipment]] for the card type.

## Action Profile [src-001]
- **Who can equip:** any ready minion.
- **Cost:** as listed on the equipment card if from hand; none if moving from another of your minions.
- **Default target:** none. **Undirected** action.
- **Default stealth:** +1.
- **Effect:**
  - **From hand:** on success, the equipment card is placed on the acting minion. No limit on how many equipment a minion can carry.
  - **Moving between your minions:** specify which equipment is being taken **when the action is announced**. More than one piece can be moved in a single action. If the action fails (blocked, etc.), equipment stays where it was.

## Discipline-Requirement Quirk (Advanced)
If equipping the card **requires a Discipline** and the equipment is put into play by **some other means** (not the equip action — e.g., a card effect that equips for free), use the **basic** version of the equipment [src-001].

## Common Cards Involved
- [.44 Magnum](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=.44%20Magnum) — if you use the maneuver from this gun during determine-range, you are committed to using its strike (2R). If you can't (e.g., Immortal Grapple), you lose the strike entirely [src-001 p. 47].
- [Magic of the Smith](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Magic%20of%20the%20Smith) — tutor; not an equip action itself; you do not search until the action succeeds, and you can choose to find no equipment [src-001 pp. 49–50].

## Common Mistakes
- Forgetting to announce which equipment is being transferred when moving between your minions.
- Using the **superior** effect of an equipment when it was placed outside the equip action (e.g., Magic of the Smith, trophy) — you must use the **basic** version in that case if the equipment required a Discipline to equip normally.
