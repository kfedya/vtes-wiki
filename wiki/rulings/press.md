# Press & End of Round

## Summary
After damage resolves, if both combatants are still **ready**, each can either go their separate ways or continue combat. A **press** is played to continue combat; a press can be cancelled by an opposing press. An **uncancelled press to continue** starts another round [src-001 pp. 32–33].

## Press Step
At the end of a round (if both combatants are still ready) [src-001 pp. 32–33]:

1. Combatants can play **presses**. Two types: press-to-continue and press-to-cancel-a-continue. They work like maneuvers — traded back and forth, offsetting each other.
2. A minion **cannot play two presses in a row** (they would cancel each other) [src-001 p. 33].
3. If there is an **uncancelled press to continue**, another round begins; otherwise combat ends.

Note: the **acting minion** still plays first at this step [src-001 p. 29].

## End of Round (Step 7)
Cards or effects usable **only at the end of a round** are played after the press step [src-001 p. 33].

- This step occurs **even if combat ends prematurely** [src-001 p. 33] — e.g., a strike sent someone to torpor.

## Single-Round Scope
A press granted by a card can only be used **the same round** it is played. It cannot be saved. The maneuver **and** press from the same card (e.g., [Resist Earth's Grasp](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Resist%20Earth%27s%20Grasp)) must be used together in that round [src-001 p. 50].

## Interactions With Other Effects
- **Hidden Strength cannot be used as a press-cycle** — it cannot be played if there is no damage to prevent, even if the player would like to trigger its press [src-001 p. 49].
- **Taste of Vitae** must be played **after** the decision to press or not [src-001 p. 51].

## Common Cards Involved
- [Resist Earth's Grasp](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Resist%20Earth%27s%20Grasp) — maneuver + press, same round only [src-001 p. 50].
- [Hidden Strength](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Hidden%20Strength) — damage-prevention with a press rider; requires damage to prevent [src-001 p. 49].
- [Taste of Vitae](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Taste%20of%20Vitae) — timed after the press decision [src-001 p. 51].
- [Freak Drive](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Freak%20Drive) — after combat unlock; can even play if the acting vampire is now in torpor, provided they have blood to pay [src-001 p. 49].

## Common Mistakes
- Playing two presses in a row with the same minion.
- Carrying a card's press to a later round.
- Skipping the end-of-round step when combat ended early.
- Attempting Hidden Strength solely to cycle into a press with no damage on the table.
