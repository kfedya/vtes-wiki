# Quick Reference

In-play cheat sheet: the symbols used on VTES cards. Reproduced from the rulebook's Quick Reference page [src-001 p. 53].

The rulebook presents each term paired with its printed **icon** (a small glyph on the physical card). The icon images are not reproduced here — identify the symbol visually and look up its meaning below.

## Disciplines (5E Core)

Nine disciplines appear on 5E crypt cards. Inferior/superior distinction: superior disciplines are marked on the card with a boxed/highlighted icon; inferior as a plain icon.

| Discipline | Notes |
|------------|-------|
| **Animalism** | Communing with and commanding beasts; retainers, environmental damage. |
| **Auspex** | Heightened perception; intercept; sense through walls. |
| **Blood Sorcery** | Thaumaturgy — glossary entry [src-001 p. 44]: "Thaumaturgy: Blood Sorcery." Ritual magic; blood manipulation. |
| **Celerity** | Supernatural speed; additional strikes; press. |
| **Dominate** | Mind control; bleed increase; [Conditioning](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Conditioning), [Deflection](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Deflection). |
| **Fortitude** | Physical resilience; damage prevention. |
| **Obfuscate** | Stealth and concealment; [Cloak the Gathering](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Cloak%20the%20Gathering), [Faceless Night](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Faceless%20Night). |
| **Potence** | Supernatural strength; hand-strike damage; [Immortal Grapple](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Immortal%20Grapple). |
| **Presence** | Charisma / supernatural allure; votes; [Bewitching Oration](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Bewitching%20Oration). |

Alternate name: **Thaumaturgy** and **Blood Sorcery** are the **same discipline** [src-001 p. 44]. Older cards may print "Thaumaturgy."

See [[entities/disciplines]] (when stubs are filled in from future krcg snapshots).

## Clans (5E Core)

Five clans are present in 5E base starter decks:

| Clan | Sect (default) |
|------|----------------|
| **Malkavian** | Camarilla. |
| **Nosferatu** | Camarilla. |
| **Toreador** | Camarilla. |
| **Tremere** | Camarilla. |
| **Ventrue** | Camarilla. |

Default sect alignment comes from the clan unless a card specifies otherwise — see [[entities/sects/camarilla]] and [[traits]] for defection mechanics (Anarch, Blood Cursed, etc.).

Other clans exist in the wider game (Brujah, Gangrel, Lasombra, Tzimisce, and many more) but are **not** in the 5E base product.
