# Discard Phase

## Summary
The **discard phase** is the fifth and final phase of a turn. By default, a Methuselah receives one **discard phase action**, which can be used to discard a card from hand and draw a replacement. Unused discard phase actions are **lost** — they cannot be saved for later [src-001 p. 37].

## Default Action
- Spend the discard phase action → discard a card from hand → draw to replace it [src-001 p. 37].

## Event Cards (Advanced)
You may use a discard phase action to put an **event card** into play, **no more than one per phase** [src-001 p. 37 Advanced].

- Some effects may change the number of discard phase actions a Methuselah receives, or grant alternate ways to spend them [src-001 p. 37 Advanced].
- **Each event card may only be played once each game** [src-001 p. 37 Advanced].

See [[library#event-cards]] for how event cards fit into the library.

## Dreams of the Sphinx — Interaction
[Dreams of the Sphinx](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Dreams%20of%20the%20Sphinx) can raise your hand size during your turn. If so, you first have the option of using your discard phase action to **discard and replace** (benefiting from the larger hand size) before the hand size shrinks back to normal by discarding 2 cards [src-001 p. 48].

## Common Mistakes
- Saving an unused discard phase action — you cannot.
- Playing more than one event card in a single discard phase.
- Replaying an event card that has already been played this game.
