# Cards Referenced But Not Included in 5E

## Summary
Some cards in the 5E base rulebook text reference other older cards that are **not included** in the Fifth Edition core product. Any card-text mention of them is a dead link for 5E play. List is from the official rulebook FAQ [src-001].

## The Cards

### Into Thin Air
- **Referenced from:** [Lost in Crowds](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Lost%20in%20Crowds).
- **Status:** not in 5E [src-001 p. 49].
- **Impact:** Lost in Crowds' printed text is still playable; ignore the cross-reference.

### Torn Signpost
- **Referenced from:** [Preternatural Strength](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Preternatural%20Strength).
- **Status:** not in 5E [src-001 p. 50].
- **Impact:** no 5E card lets you bring in Torn Signpost via Preternatural Strength.

### Minion Tap
- **Referenced from:** [Villein](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Villein).
- **Status:** not in 5E [src-001 p. 52].
- **Impact:** Villein is functional on its own (see [[master-phase#villein]]); the Minion Tap reference is vestigial.

## Implications
- These cards may still appear in a mixed-edition playgroup if older expansions are in play. In a pure 5E context they cannot be tutored, drawn, or triggered.
- Printed references in card text do **not** disqualify the containing card — the underlying card works under its own rules.
