# Strike (Choose & Resolve)

## Summary
The strike step is where combatants actually swing (or avoid swinging). Each minion normally gets **one strike per round**, chosen by the acting minion first, then by the opposing minion, and the two strikes are resolved **simultaneously**. The pair of strikes may be followed by **additional strikes** granted by cards or effects [src-001 pp. 29–31].

## Strike Step Flow
1. **Before strikes are chosen** — any cards playable only after range is set but before strikes have been chosen are played now [src-001 p. 30].
2. **Choose Strike** — acting minion chooses first, then the opposing minion. A strike may come from:
   - a combat card,
   - a weapon the minion possesses,
   - the default **hand strike** (see [[strike-effects#hand-strike]]),
   - any other card providing this minion a strike.
   If the minion already used a **maneuver from a strike card or weapon** this round, they cannot choose **any other** strike for their initial strike of the round [src-001 p. 30].
3. **Resolve Strike** — both strikes resolve simultaneously, with ordering exceptions for combat ends and first strike (see [[strike-effects]]) [src-001 p. 30].

## "Pair" of Strikes
One strike from each minion is a **pair** [src-001 p. 30]. Pair resolution is simultaneous. Damage applies immediately when a minion or retainer takes damage, even mid-resolution — if one combatant is no longer ready, the round and combat end at once [src-001 p. 30].

## Dodges Are Strikes
Defensive strikes such as **dodge** count as strikes; they simply deal no damage. They represent the minion's activity during that pair of strikes [src-001 pp. 29, 33].

## Additional Strikes
Some cards or effects give a minion **extra strikes** the same round. Rules [src-001 pp. 30–31]:
- Additional strikes happen **after** the normal pair.
- If only one minion has additional strikes, they resolve on their own (no pair).
- Acting minion decides whether to take additional strikes **before** the opposing minion, as usual.
- Additional strikes all occur at the **same range** as the normal pair.
- A minion may choose **any** strike for an additional strike — even if they used a strike-card or weapon maneuver earlier [src-001 p. 30 Advanced].
- **Limited**: a minion cannot use more than one card or effect to gain additional strikes per round of combat [src-001 p. 30].

### Cancelled Strike Card — Choose Again (Advanced)
If a strike **card** is cancelled, the minion who played it must choose a strike, which might come from another strike card [src-001 p. 31].

> This is distinct from strike cards used via a **maneuver**: those commit the minion to that strike; if cancelled, the minion cannot strike at all this round. See [[maneuver#strike-card--weapon-maneuver-commitment]] and [.44 Magnum](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=.44%20Magnum) [src-001 p. 47].

## Example (from rulebook)
Wauneka is blocked by Flávio Gonçalves. Neither maneuvers, so close range. Wauneka strikes with Roundhouse (4 damage). Flávio Gonçalves uses his special ability to dodge. No damage is dealt.

Wauneka plays a card giving 1 additional strike. Flávio Gonçalves plays a card giving 2 additional strikes. Both additional strikes are at close range.

Second strike: Wauneka uses hand strike; Flávio Gonçalves uses hand strike. Both take 1 damage.

Third strike: Flávio Gonçalves uses his hand strike one more time; Wauneka has no further additional strikes. Wauneka takes 1 more damage [src-001 p. 31].

## Edge Cases
- **Roundhouse** is a hand strike and therefore works under [Immortal Grapple](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Immortal%20Grapple) [src-001 p. 50].
- **Carrion Crows** damage resolves at the same time as regular strikes and **cannot be dodged**, since dodge only protects from the opponent's **strike** [src-001 p. 48].
- **Murder of Crows** inflicts environmental damage from the retainer; otherwise like Carrion Crows [src-001 p. 49].

## Common Cards Involved
- [Roundhouse](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Roundhouse) — hand-strike compatible with Immortal Grapple [src-001 p. 50].
- [Carrion Crows](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Carrion%20Crows) — strike-timed environmental damage, not dodgeable [src-001 p. 48].
- [Immortal Grapple](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Immortal%20Grapple) — closes strikes to hand strikes; played after maneuver step [src-001 p. 49].

## Common Mistakes
- Treating a dodge as "not a strike" and therefore allowing a second strike that round.
- Letting a minion take additional strikes from two different cards in the same round.
- Choosing a new strike after a weapon-maneuver strike is cancelled.
- Allowing additional strikes at a different range than the pair.
