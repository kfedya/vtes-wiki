# Politics — Overview

## Summary
"Politics and diplomacy are as critical to vampires as hunting and feeding." Politics is the umbrella for **political actions** (vampires calling referendums) and **blood hunts** (automatic referendums after diablerie) [src-001].

## The Three Moving Parts
1. **The Political Action** — a vampire-only action that, if successful, calls a referendum. See [[political-action]].
2. **The Referendum** — the voting procedure itself: choose terms → polling → resolve. See [[referendum]].
3. **Votes and Ballots** — how Methuselahs accumulate voting power. See [[voting]].

## Key Cross-Cutting Rules
- Political actions are **always undirected**. Prey and predator decide blocks [src-001 p. 26].
- The referendum result is **independent** of the political action's success. A referendum can fail while the action is fully successful [src-001 p. 47].
- Blood hunt referendums **cannot be blocked** and **cannot be responded to with action modifiers or reaction cards** [src-001 p. 35].
- Locked vampires **can vote** if they are ready; torpor vampires **cannot** [src-001 pp. 28, 34].
