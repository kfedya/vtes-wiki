# Become Anarch

## Summary
An **Advanced Rules** action by any ready **untitled non-Anarch** vampire to change their sect to Anarch [src-001]. Changing sect means they lose any residual sect-specific benefits; future title eligibility follows the new sect (see [[voting]] and future sect pages).

## Action Profile [src-001]
- **Who can become Anarch:** any ready **untitled** **non-Anarch** vampire.
- **Cost:** 2 blood, or **1 blood** if the controller already controls at least 1 other ready Anarch.
- **Default target:** none. **Undirected** action.
- **Default stealth:** +1.
- **Effect:** on success, the acting vampire is now **Anarch**. This is a **sect change** [src-001 p. 40].

## Sect-Change Implications [src-001 p. 39]
- A vampire always belongs to exactly one sect. If they change sect, they no longer belong to the old one.
- They cannot have more than one title; changing sect can leave a titled vampire with a title unsupported by their new sect, in which case they **lose the benefit** of the title until their sect changes back (or they acquire a matching title).
- The "Become Anarch" action requires the vampire be **untitled**, so this particular flow sidesteps the title-loss issue.

## Other Ways to Become Anarch
Some card effects also convert a vampire to the Anarch sect. These count as sect changes and follow the same sect rules [src-001 p. 40].

## Common Mistakes
- Trying to become Anarch with a titled vampire — the action requires the vampire to be untitled.
- Forgetting the 1-blood discount when you already have a ready Anarch on the board.
- Assuming a title carries over across sect changes — it does not; benefit is lost until the sect aligns again.
