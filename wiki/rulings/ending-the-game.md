# Ending the Game

## Summary
A Methuselah is **ousted** when they run out of pool counters. When your **prey** is ousted (regardless of who caused it), you gain **1 victory point** and **6 pool** from the blood bank. The game ends when one Methuselah remains; the winner is the Methuselah with the most victory points, even if they've been ousted [src-001 p. 37].

## On Being Ousted
When a Methuselah is ousted [src-001 p. 37]:
- All the cards they control are **removed from the game**.
- Any cards of rivals they controlled are **returned to their owners** at the end of the game.
- Any of their cards controlled by other Methuselahs **remain in play** as normal.

The game continues until only one Methuselah is left.

## Rewards for Ousting Your Prey
When your prey is ousted, you gain [src-001 p. 37]:
- **1 victory point**, and
- **6 pool counters** from the blood bank.

This happens **no matter how or by whom** your prey was ousted.

You receive **an additional victory point** if you are the **last Methuselah** remaining.

## Simultaneous Ouster Exception
If a Methuselah is ousted **at the same time** that their prey is ousted, that Methuselah gets the **victory point** but does **not** gain 6 pool counters [src-001 p. 38].

This extends further via the FAQ: an action can result in multiple Methuselahs being ousted at the same time. Every Methuselah whose prey is ousted (even if they themselves are ousted) gains **1 VP**, but **only those Methuselahs who survive the action** gain pool for their prey's ouster [src-001 p. 46].

## Prey Shift After Ouster
When your prey is ousted, the next Methuselah to your left (the ousted Methuselah's prey) becomes your new prey, and you become their new predator [src-001 p. 38].

## Example (from rulebook, p. 38)
Justine, Lisa, Richard, Scott, and Steve sit clockwise around the table in that order.
- Steve is ousted first → Scott (Steve's predator) gains 6 pool and 1 VP.
- Scott is ousted next by Richard → Richard gains 6 pool and 1 VP (Scott is Richard's prey now).
- Lisa is ousted next → Justine gains 6 pool and 1 VP.
- Justine and Richard remain, each other's prey and predator.
- Richard falls; Justine gets 6 pool and 1 VP.
- Justine was not ousted at all → bonus 1 VP.
- Final: Justine wins with 3 VP; Richard and Scott each with 1 VP; Lisa and Steve with 0.

## Determining the Winner
At the end of the game the winner is the Methuselah with the **most victory points**, even if they have been ousted [src-001 p. 37]. In the case of a tie, there is **no winner** [src-001 p. 37].

## Alternative: Withdrawal
A Methuselah with an exhausted library can choose to **withdraw** during unlock phase — see [[withdraw]] [src-001 p. 38 Advanced].

## Self-Ouster Edge Case
- [[wiki/cards/smiling-jack-the-anarch|Smiling Jack, The Anarch]] forces its controller to move 1 pool to the card in their unlock phase — **even if doing so ousts them** [src-001 p. 51]. This is the canonical case where a Methuselah ousts themselves directly and immediately.

## Common Mistakes
- Awarding 6 pool to a predator who was ousted by the same action as their prey — they get the VP, not the pool.
- Assuming the last-Methuselah bonus VP requires winning outright — it only requires being the last remaining.
- Forgetting to remove an ousted Methuselah's controlled rival cards and return them at end of game.
- Letting a Smiling Jack controller skip the 1-pool payment on the turn it would oust them — they cannot.
