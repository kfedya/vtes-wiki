# Traits

## Summary
**Traits** are attributes minions can have that interact with other game effects — they let minions play cards that require a specific trait, or be affected by cards that care about that trait. Some traits are listed on card text; some come from card effects in play [src-001 p. 40]. There may be other traits beyond those listed below [src-001 p. 41].

## Black Hand
Allows a minion to play or use cards requiring **Black Hand** minions [src-001 p. 41].

## Blood Cursed
A vampire who is **Blood Cursed cannot commit diablerie** [src-001 p. 41]. See [[diablerie]].

## Circle
Each **Blood Brother** is identified with a particular **circle**. A vampire without a circle designation is their **own** circle. **Inner Circle** (the Camarilla title) is not a Blood Brother circle [src-001 p. 41].

## Flight
Allows a minion to play or use cards requiring **Flight** [src-001 p. 41].

## Infernal
An infernal minion **does not unlock as normal** during the unlock phase. During its controller's unlock phase, they may **burn 1 pool** to unlock that infernal minion [src-001 p. 41].

## Red List
- Any Methuselah may use a **master phase action** to mark a Red List minion for the **current turn** [src-001 p. 41].
- Any ready vampire they control may enter combat with a marked Red List minion as a **+1 stealth** action that costs **1 blood**.
- Each vampire can perform this action **only once each turn** [src-001 p. 41].
- If a vampire **burns** a Red List minion in combat or as a **directed action** (including diablerie), their controller may search their library, ash heap, and/or hand for a **master trophy card** to put on that vampire, then reshuffle their library or draw back up to hand size.
- Other trophies in play that have not been awarded yet may be moved to this vampire as well, as each trophy's controller wishes.
- This happens **before** the blood hunt referendum is called, if any [src-001 p. 41].

## Scarce
When a Methuselah moves a **scarce** vampire from their uncontrolled region to their ready region during their influence phase, they must **burn 3 pool for each vampire of the same clan** they already control [src-001 p. 41].

## Slave
Some minions are **slaves** to a specified clan. Restrictions [src-001 p. 41]:
- A slave cannot perform a **directed action** if their controller does not control a **ready member of the specified clan**.
- If a member of the specified clan controlled by the same Methuselah is **blocked**, the controller can **lock the slave** to:
  - **cancel the combat**,
  - **unlock the acting vampire**, and
  - have the slave enter combat with the blocking minion instead.

## Sterile
**Sterile** vampires cannot perform actions to put new vampires in play [src-001 p. 41].

## Common Mistakes
- Unlocking an Infernal minion for free in the unlock phase — requires 1 pool per unlock.
- Marking a Red List minion more than once per turn per vampire (the +1 stealth combat is once-per-vampire-per-turn).
- Forgetting the scarce 3-pool penalty per same-clan vampire.
- Forgetting the slave restriction on directed actions.
- Treating Inner Circle as a Blood Brother circle — it isn't.
