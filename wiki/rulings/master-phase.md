# Master Phase

## Summary
The phase in which the Methuselah takes their personal activity — typically **one master phase action** per turn. Not used = lost; master phase actions cannot be saved [src-001].

## Step-by-Step Sequence
1. Count your master phase actions. Default is **1**. Some cards modify this count.
2. Play master cards and/or use other effects that consume master phase actions, in the order you choose.
3. When you are done, proceed to the minion phase.

## Uses of a Master Phase Action
- Play a **regular master card** (see [[master]]).
- Play an alternate effect that explicitly spends a master phase action.
- **Mark a Red List minion** for the current turn (Advanced) [src-001].

## Out-of-Turn Masters (Advanced)
An **out-of-turn master** card is played during **another Methuselah's** master phase, using your *next* master phase action [src-001].

- If you have played an out-of-turn master against this master phase (even if it was **cancelled**), you gain **one fewer** master phase action than normal next turn.
- A Methuselah can never play more than one out-of-turn master between two of their turns, even if they regain a master phase action through other effects [src-001 glossary p. 43].

## Sequencing with Other Effects
If other effects trigger during the master phase (e.g., an Academic Hunting Ground produces blood), **you choose the order** in which effects and master phase actions resolve [src-001 p. 18].

## Common Cards Involved
- [Academic Hunting Ground](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Academic%20Hunting%20Ground) — master card; waits until next turn to produce blood; gaining blood is **optional** [src-001 p. 47]. Hunting grounds generally follow this pattern.
- [Vessel](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Vessel) — master card; moves blood starting next turn, once per unlock [src-001 p. 51].
- [Blood Doll](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Blood%20Doll) — master card; can be used on the **same** turn it is played (triggers in master phase, not unlock) [src-001 p. 47].
- [Anarch Troublemaker](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=Anarch%20Troublemaker) — master card; effect triggers next turn; "up to two" includes zero — useful for transferring control [src-001 p. 47].

## Common Mistakes
- Using a master card on the turn it was played when the card actually says it triggers in a later phase (most hunting grounds, Vessel).
- Forgetting to reduce next turn's master phase actions after an opponent plays an out-of-turn master against you — you must reduce yours, not theirs.
- Trying to bank an unused master phase action — you can't.
