# Banu Haqim

## Overview

Banu Haqim is the V5 rebrand of the legacy **Assamite** clan, and is V5-playable [src-001 p. 53]. The V5 signature discipline set is Blood Sorcery, Celerity, and Obfuscate, reflecting a shift away from the legacy Quietus-centered identity toward sorcerous judge / enforcer flavor. Card-db evidence: of 83 Banu Haqim vampires (the snapshot has fully renamed the legacy Assamite roster under this name), `CEL` (49), `QUI` (46), and `OBF` (39) dominate superior disciplines; `THA` (Blood Sorcery, 16) is rising from V5 printings [src-002].

## V5 rebrand

The card-db has consolidated all former "Assamite" and "Assamite antitribu" crypt entries under "Banu Haqim" (0 crypt entries remain under the legacy names). Legacy library cards still reference "Assamite" in text — 31 library cards match on name/text — and those cards remain legal. V5 printings adopt Blood Sorcery in place of Quietus as the flagship discipline, though legacy Quietus-heavy crypt still dominates raw counts.

## Sect alignment

Nearly even split between **Independent** (7) and **Camarilla** (7) on printed sect text, with 1 **Sabbat**, 1 **Laibon**, and 1 **Anarch** [src-002]. Legacy Assamite lore is Independent (Alamut-aligned); V5 lore has a faction of Banu Haqim joining the Camarilla as its enforcers.

## Typical deck archetypes

- **Banu Haqim stealth-bleed** — OBF stealth modifiers + CEL reactions; bleeds through with cheap Quietus combat for defense.
- **Sorcerer-Assassin (V5 direction)** — THA ranged combat + CEL reactions; blood-sorcery rituals replacing legacy Quietus poisons.
- **Legacy Quietus combat** — QUI combat cards (Silent Death, Taste of Death) paired with CEL/OBF; still strong given crypt depth.
