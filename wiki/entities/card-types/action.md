# Action Cards

## Summary
An **action card** is a library minion card that lets a **ready, unlocked** minion perform an action other than the default actions [src-001]. Played by vampires or allies during the **minion phase**.

## Core Rules
- Only **one** action card can be played per action.
- Action cards **cannot** be used to modify other actions — that's what [[action-modifier]] cards are for.
- The minion must meet the card's requirements (clan, Discipline level, capacity, title, cost, etc.) — see [[library#Requirements for Playing Cards]].
- Playing an action card **locks** the minion (the action itself locks them).

## Default Actions (for reference)
Some actions do not require an action card — every minion can attempt them:
- **Bleed** the prey (reduce their pool by 1 before blocks/modifiers).
- **Block** an action against you or another Methuselah you defend.
- Other minion-specific abilities printed on the crypt card.

Full default-action catalog belongs in the Minion Phase ruling — stage 3 ingest.

## Flow (abbreviated)
1. Announce the action by playing the card and its effects.
2. Card is temporarily out of play until resolution [src-001].
3. Other Methuselahs may attempt to **block** with a ready unlocked minion (see [[reaction]] for cards played in this window).
4. If unblocked, the action resolves. If blocked, combat may follow (see [[combat-card]]).
5. On resolution, the action card goes to the ash heap (unless the card says otherwise).

Full bleed/block/combat mechanics arrive in the stage 3 (minion phase) and stage 4 (combat) ingests.

## Cancellation
If an action card is **cancelled**, the minion does **not** lock (they didn't pay for the action card) and they can play the same action card again [src-001].
