# Combat Cards

## Summary
A **combat card** is a library minion card played by a minion **during combat** [src-001 p. 12]. The only minion cards playable during combat are combat cards [src-001 p. 29]. This page covers the *card type*; for combat mechanics see [[combat]], [[maneuver]], [[strike]], [[strike-effects]], [[damage-resolution]], [[press]].

## Core Rules (card-level)
- Combat cards are only playable inside a combat — i.e., after a block has resolved successfully and combat has begun, or when a card causes combat (see [[combat]]).
- The **acting minion** plays cards first at every step of combat; the opposing minion responds [src-001 p. 29].
- Cost and Discipline requirements apply as with any library card — see [[library#requirements-for-playing-cards]].
- Some combat cards are played by minions **not involved in the current combat** — those can be played by minions controlled by **any** Methuselah [src-001 p. 29 Advanced].

## Sub-Roles Within Combat
Combat cards fulfil various roles that plug into the 7-step round sequence [src-001 pp. 29–33]:

- **Maneuver** — set range during step 2. See [[maneuver]].
- **Strike / strike effect** — chosen in step 4. See [[strike]] and [[strike-effects]] (hand strike, dodge, combat ends, steal blood, destroy/steal equipment, first strike).
- **Damage prevention** — played during step 5 prevent phase. See [[damage-resolution]].
- **Press** — used in step 6 to continue or end combat. See [[press]].
- **End-of-round / before-range / before-strikes** — timed to the matching combat step. See [[combat]].

## Cancellation
> If a combat card used for a strike is cancelled, the minion who played it must choose a strike which may come from another strike card. [src-001 p. 31]

In other words: cancelling a strike card forces the attacker to pick a replacement strike — either a default strike (e.g., hand strike) or another strike card.

**Exception — weapon / strike-card maneuver commitment:** if the cancelled strike came from a maneuver used with a weapon or strike card, the minion **cannot** pick another strike — they simply lose their strike for the round. See [[maneuver#strike-card--weapon-maneuver-commitment]] and [.44 Magnum](https://codex-of-the-damned.org/en/card-search/library/index.html?card=.44%20Magnum) [src-001 p. 47].

## "(limited)" Rule
- A minion cannot use **more than one** card or effect to gain **additional strikes** per round of combat [src-001 p. 30].
- This is reminded on the cards by "(limited)" card text.
