# Ally Cards

## Summary
An **ally** is a library minion card put into play as an **independent minion** (not attached to another card) with its **starting life from the blood bank** [src-001]. Allies are the non-vampire minions under a Methuselah's control — mortals, ghouls, animals, and other supernaturals.

## Core Rules
- Played as an action (Advanced) by a ready unlocked minion (usually by the Methuselah's first vampire) or via a master-card-like play method depending on the card.
- On resolution, the ally goes into the ready region with its printed starting life drawn from the blood bank.
- An ally's **life** serves the same physical role as blood on a vampire — it's the currency for costs, damage tracking, etc. [src-001].
- Allies are **ready, unlockable minions**: they can take actions, block, and play minion cards within the limits their card text allows.

## "As a Vampire" Rule (Advanced)
Some allies have the ability to play certain cards **as a vampire**. In those cases [src-001]:
- The ally is treated as a vampire for all effects generated by **playing that card**, including duration effects like "for the remainder of combat".
- Their **life** counts as **blood** for costs, gains, and losses.
- The ally has a **capacity of 1** for capacity-based requirements on that card.
- If the card would send the ally to **torpor**, the ally is **burned** instead.
- Aggravated damage inflicted on the ally as a vampire still burns life as normal — life and blood aren't exactly interchangeable in all respects, but life capacity isn't a ceiling (gained life beyond capacity doesn't drain off).

**Crucially:** the "as a vampire" treatment only applies to the effect **generated from playing the card**. It does **not** apply to *in-play* effects like "the vampire with this card" [src-001].

## Lifecycle
- Take damage during combat or via action cards → burn life.
- Life reaches 0 → the ally is burned.
- The controlling Methuselah is ousted → allies go with them (like other minions).
