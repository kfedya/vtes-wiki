# Master Cards

## Summary
Master cards are library cards played by the **Methuselah**, not by minions. They have no icon at the top of the attribute bar [src-001]. You play them during your **master phase** as master phase actions.

## Two Categories

### Regular Master Cards
Played during your own master phase, one per master phase action. At the start of each master phase, you get **one** master phase action (trifles and out-of-turn plays can change this — see below).

### Out-of-Turn Master Cards
Can only be played during **another Methuselah's turn**. Playing one uses a master phase action **from your next master phase** — even if the out-of-turn card is cancelled [src-001].

Constraints:
- Regardless of how many master phase actions you normally have, you cannot play a **second** out-of-turn master card before your next master phase.
- You cannot play an out-of-turn master card on **your own** turn.

## Control
A master card in play is controlled by the **Methuselah who played it**, even if the card is placed on a minion controlled by another Methuselah [src-001].

## Master Card Sub-Types

### Locations
Represent a place (building, city, gathering place). Stay in play and can be used **repeatedly**, even on the turn they're played. Some effects can burn them [src-001].

### Trifles
Identified by the word "Trifle" in the card text. When a Methuselah successfully plays a trifle:
- **Regular trifle** → gain an **additional master phase action** this turn.
- **Out-of-turn trifle** → gain a master phase action in your **next** master phase.

Cap: only **one** master phase action from trifles per master phase — any subsequent trifles act like regular master cards [src-001].

### Trophies (Advanced)
Put into play using a master phase action (or retrieved via the **Red List** trait — see rulebook p. 41). No effect until moved onto a vampire. Once placed:
- Controlled by the controller of the vampire it is on.
- Cannot be awarded again [src-001].

### Discipline Cards (Advanced)
Played on a controlled vampire to give them an **additional level** of a Discipline:
- Basic level of a new Discipline, or
- Basic → superior on an existing Discipline.

Most Discipline cards also **increase the vampire's capacity by +1** (indicated by a "+1" in the red circle in the lower-right). The vampire does **not** automatically gain an extra blood to fill the new capacity [src-001].

### Other Master Cards
Any master card not in the categories above has its effects described on the card itself [src-001].

## Common Gotchas
- Out-of-turn trifles **still count** against next turn's master allotment, even if cancelled.
- A cancelled master card is still "played" and still pays its cost — see [[game-overview#Wording Templates]] on cancel-a-card rules.
