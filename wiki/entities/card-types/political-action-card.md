# Political Action Cards

## Summary
A **political action card** can be played to call a **referendum** as an action, or **burned during a referendum to gain a vote** [src-001].

## Core Rules
- **Only vampires** can play political action cards — allies cannot [src-001].
- Playing one as an action calls a referendum; the full referendum/voting/title mechanics arrive in stage 3 (minion phase) ingest. Full glossary and title-vote counts are on rulebook p. 27 (to be extracted later).
- Political action cards in hand can also be **burned during another Methuselah's referendum** to contribute **1 vote**, without acting on them — they function as a vote currency.

## Two Uses in One Card
| Mode | Who | When | Effect |
|---|---|---|---|
| As an action | The acting vampire | Minion phase | Call the referendum printed on the card |
| As a vote | Any Methuselah | During any referendum | Burn from hand for +1 vote |

## Cancellation
If the political action is cancelled as an **action card**, the vampire does **not** lock and can play the same card again [src-001]. Cost is not paid (cancel-action rule).
