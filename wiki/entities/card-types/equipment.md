# Equipment Cards

## Summary
An **equipment card** is a library minion card played **on the acting minion** [src-001]. The minion wearing the equipment is called the **bearer**.

## Core Rules
- Played as an action by the acting minion (typically called an *equip* action).
- Put into play on the bearer on resolution.
- Stays in play until the bearer is burned or the equipment is explicitly burned by an effect.
- **Burned when** the bearer is burned [src-001].
- Equipment persists through torpor by default — a vampire sent to torpor keeps their equipment (unless an effect says otherwise). Equipment-moving and -stealing rules belong in the action-cards ingest stage.

## Versus Retainers
- **Equipment** — inanimate gear; no life; burned with bearer.
- **Retainer** — living minion-on-minion; has life; burned when life hits 0 or bearer is burned.
