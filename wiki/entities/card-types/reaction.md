# Reaction Cards

## Summary
A **reaction card** is played by a **ready, unlocked** minion in response to an action taken by a minion controlled by **another Methuselah** [src-001].

## Core Rules
- Playable only in response to another Methuselah's action. **Not** playable on your own action.
- The minion must be **ready and unlocked**.
- A reaction card **does not lock** the minion playing it [src-001].
- A minion **cannot play the same reaction card more than once** during a single action — even at a different Discipline level [src-001].

## Common Uses (examples only)
- Attempt to block (some blocks require reaction cards for stealth-beating).
- Wake up a locked minion with a **wake effect** so it can interact (rulebook p. 44 glossary — full wake ruling TBD).
- Gain intercept, deflect actions, draw cards off an action.

## Flow
Reaction cards live in the impulse window after an action is announced and before it resolves. See [[game-overview#Sequencing (advanced)]] for the order of impulse in a directed action.

## Cancellation
Non-action cards that are cancelled still **pay their cost** [src-001]. Reaction cards themselves can be cancelled by reflex effects or other cards.
