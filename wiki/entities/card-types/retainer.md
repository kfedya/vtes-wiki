# Retainer Cards

## Summary
A **retainer** is a library minion card played **on the acting minion**, bringing with it a starting pool of **life** drawn from the blood bank [src-001]. The minion the retainer is attached to is called the **employer**.

## Core Rules
- Put into play on the acting minion on resolution of the play action.
- Retainer starts with its printed life from the blood bank.
- A retainer is **burned when**:
  - Its last life is burned, **or**
  - Its **employer** (the minion it is on) is burned [src-001].
- Life on a retainer soaks damage directed at the retainer; some retainers have abilities that trigger on damage, on entry, or as ongoing effects.

## Versus Allies
- **Retainer** — attached to a minion; burned when the employer is burned; effects typically augment the bearer.
- **Ally** — independent minion; can act on its own; separate from the Methuselah's vampires.
