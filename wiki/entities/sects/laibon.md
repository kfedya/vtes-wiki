# Laibon (Legacy)

## Summary
The **Laibon** is a legacy vampire sect from sets released prior to VTES Fifth Edition. Only Laibon can hold the laibon titles **kholo** and **magaji** [src-001 p. 40].

> **Legacy Note:** The rules for "Other Vampire Sects" — Sabbat and Laibon — relate to cards released in sets prior to VTES Fifth Edition. These sets are fully compatible with 5E [src-001 p. 40].

## Titles and Votes
During the polling step of a referendum, a Methuselah gains **2 votes** for each ready **kholo** or **magaji** they control [src-001 p. 40].

## Uniqueness and Contests
- The **magaji** title is **not unique** and **cannot be contested** [src-001 p. 40].
- **Kholo** titles are **clan kholo** — each kholo can only be held by a vampire of the **appropriate clan**, and each is **unique to that clan** [src-001 p. 40].

## Title Loss on Sect/Clan Change
Same rule as Camarilla — see [[camarilla#title-loss-on-sectclan-change]] [src-001 p. 39].
