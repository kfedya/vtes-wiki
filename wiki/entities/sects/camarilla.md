# Camarilla

## Summary
The **Camarilla** is the default, established vampire sect in VTES. Only Camarilla vampires can hold the Camarilla titles: **primogen**, **prince**, **justicar**, and **Inner Circle member**. Each grants a different number of votes during referendums. Some titles are unique; some are tied to a clan or a city [src-001 p. 39].

## Titles and Votes
During the polling step of a referendum, a Methuselah gains [src-001 pp. 27–28]:
- **1 vote** for each ready **primogen**
- **2 votes** for each ready **prince** or **baron** (baron is Anarch — see [[anarch]])
- **3 votes** for each ready **justicar**
- **4 votes** for each ready **Inner Circle member**

## Uniqueness and Contests
- Each clan's **justicar** and **Inner Circle** titles are **unique** and can only be held by vampires **of that clan** [src-001 p. 39]. Contests on these are paid per [[block-resolution#contests]] / titles rules.
- The title of **prince** is associated with a **particular city** and can be contested by another vampire who claims any title to the same city [src-001 p. 39].
- The title of **primogen** is **not unique** and **cannot be contested** [src-001 p. 39].

## Title Loss on Sect/Clan Change
A vampire must belong to the appropriate sect to receive a title. If a vampire with a title changes clan or sect to one inappropriate for the title, they **lose the benefit** of the title until their clan/sect becomes appropriate again. If they gain a new title, or their title is contested, they immediately yield the old title [src-001 p. 39].

If a vampire with a title gains another title, they **lose the first** even if the new title would be a demotion [src-001 p. 39].
