# Independent

## Summary
Any vampire not aligned with any of the major sects is an **Independent**. Independent vampires are identified simply as "Independent" in card text. Some have printed votes, treated as titles of their own not tied to a sect [src-001 p. 39].

## Printed Votes as Title-Less Titles
Some Independent vampires start with **votes**, as listed on card text. Treat these vampires as if they had titles of their own. These titles are **not tied to a sect** [src-001 p. 39].

## Legacy Status
Independent is grouped with the "Other Vampire Sects" section of the rulebook and relates to cards from sets released prior to Fifth Edition [src-001 p. 40]. These cards remain fully compatible with 5E.
