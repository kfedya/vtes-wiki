# Anarch

## Summary
**Anarch** is a vampire sect. Some vampires are Anarch by default (as written on their card); any untitled non-Anarch vampire can also **become Anarch** via a +1 stealth undirected action. The only Anarch title is **baron** [src-001 p. 39].

## Becoming Anarch
See [[become-anarch]] for the action profile. Summary [src-001 p. 39]:
- **Untitled non-Anarch** vampire only.
- **2 blood** cost, or **1 blood** if the controller controls at least **1 other ready Anarch**.
- **+1 stealth**, **undirected**.
- A vampire can also be made Anarch by certain card effects.

Becoming Anarch constitutes a **change of sect**, which applies the standard title-loss rules (see [[camarilla#title-loss-on-sectclan-change]]) [src-001 p. 39]. Being Anarch has no effect on game play except as defined by cards and effects in play. Some cards can only be played by Anarch vampires [src-001 p. 39].

## Title: Baron
**Baron** is a title that can only be held by an Anarch [src-001 p. 39]:
- Associated with a **particular city**.
- Can be **contested** by another vampire who claims the title of **prince**, **archbishop**, or **baron** of the same city (cross-sect city contest).
- If a baron changes sect, they **lose the benefit** of the title until they become Anarch again, as usual for titles [src-001 p. 39].

## Votes
During the polling step of a referendum, a Methuselah gains **2 votes** for each ready **baron** (grouped with prince in the rulebook's vote table) [src-001 p. 27].
