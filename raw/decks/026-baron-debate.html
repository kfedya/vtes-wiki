o Baron Debate

VDB

Highlights

This is a wall deck using Brujah o Barons and Brujah Debate. It aims to have more minions by influencing them and using Childe of the Revolution, and stack and defend the Brujah Debate with Organized Resistance

The deck can put some pressure forward with naked bleeds, but the oust is supported by the typical wall devices Smiling Jack, The Anarch and Constant Revolution.

Stacking Brujah Debate is the goal, but should not be started too soon: at least 3 minions should be out, including a second Baron, before immobilizing the highest Baron with the debate. Villein is used to recoup pool and influence more vampires.

Aline Gadëke's ability is particularily useful here and her being the highest capacity of the crypt is important: she is the best Brujah Debate "victim" when available.

As the game progresses and the Brujah Debate start to stack, each minion becomes a threat: with huge strength and multiple maneuvers, they can take down anyone. The combat module piles on this already dreadful prospect, with Dust Up to hit harder and dodge, and Immortal Grapple against combat ends.

Tips & Tricks

Aranthebes is included for additional intercept, but also to contest another one on the board if needed.

Open War is a strong potential game changer, finding the right opportunity to get it through unblocked is tricky.

Life Boon is a typical wall technique too: it can be used to save the grand-prey, or any other Methuselah really, to prevent opponents lunges and control the table fold better.

Having multiple Master phase actions is key to pile the Brujah Debate faster and still be able to play some other Master cards. Hence The Parthenon and Rumors of Gehenna.

Variants

This is a bit of a new thing, many have tried but few won with the idea, so there are not really variants yet. A bit more permanent intercept to support the Organized Resistance strategy could make sense, like adding Mr. Winthrop and/or a Sport Bike. Consanguineous Boon can be include to be able to bloat a bit, like in José Felipe's deck.
