Highlights

This is a stealth & bleed starring Thucimia, helped by a couple of lower capacity sidekicks.

The idea is to influence Thucimia, and bleed while also continuously stacking more permanent bleed enhancers on her thanks to a second action from Freak Drive, Dabbler and Forced March.

Bleed power is ludicrous. e or q are used for bleeding with Kindred Spirits and Loss, and stealth is provided mostly by Obfuscate O modifiers, but also by Confusion.

Between her ability and Learjet, Thucimia allows for efficient cycling, so she can equip with bleed enhancers like Dive into Madness, J.S. Simmons, Esq., Tasha Morgan, and most importantly Enkil Cog. The Enkil Cog is key here, as it allos Thucimia to bleed

Tips & Tricks

The key tactic of the deck is to use Enkil Cog to bleed during the prey's turn, thus ensuring they cannot use Archon Investigation. Then boost Thucimia's bleed as much as possible, and use Deed The Heart's Desire, Direct Intervention and Touch Of Clarity to prevent bounces.

Secure Haven is the only protection Thucimia can get from rushes, apart from sacrificing another vampire to the block.

Not only can Direct Intervention be used during an Enkil Cog bleed in another Methuselah's turn, it is also a safeguard to cancel any potential contesting Enkil Cog on the table.

Wash can be used to counter permanent intercept like Krcg News Radio, as well as bloat Master cards like Villein and, maybe more importantly, any tentative to contest Secure Haven.

Variants

There is no variant to this deck.
